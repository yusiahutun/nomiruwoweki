Sienna Miller has always been known for her ability to make a fashion statement.

And once again the 31-year-old English actress did not disappoint with her wardrobe choice for both the 2013 British Fashion Awards and Playboy's 60th anniversary party, when she arrived in an emerald gown, with a leg-revealing split. 

The star, with her newly dyed pink hair, was first spotted at the BFA's at the London Coliseum with her fiancé Tom Sturridge, before moving on to the Playboy party on Monday evening. 

Leggy lady: Sienna Miller shows bit of her leg as she arrives for the British Fashion Awards on Monday evening

Sienna's reveling outfit showed off her slender and incredibly toned legs to perfection, as she sauntered in and out of the different parties.

The Alfie actress teamed her silk Burberry gown with a white shearling jacket and a pair of Vintage strappy Burberry silver heels, a Roland Mouret gold clutch, chunky silver circular Dominic Jones necklace and drop earrings.

Her newly pink highlighted blonde tresses was left in her signature bohemian waves whilst keeping her make-up in a similar stripped back style, wearing just a touch of black eyeliner, mascara, cheek highlighter and nude lipstick. 

Lots of leg: Sienna made sure that she accentuated her best features in a stunning emerald green gown

Sienna, who is currently filming American comedy Business Trip and is set to star in Drama Mississippi Grind, attended both events, like many other celebrities including Rita Ora and Kate Moss on the evening.

Accompanying the star for the evening was her fiancé Tom Sturridge, and both put on a very stylish display as they made a rare red carpet appearance together.

Adding to the fashion credentials, the pair cemented their standing in the sartorial world when they became the joint faces of Burberry earlier this year.

Poised: Sienna Miller and Christopher Bailey posed up next to each other at the British Fashion Awards, at the London Coliseum

Cute couple: Sienna Miller and Tom Sturridge cuddle up at the Playboy 60th anniversary party at the Playboy Club in London

Escorted off: Sienna is escorted into a taxi by some bouncers outside the British Fashion Awards

And at the BFAs she was even asked to take to the stage to present Brand of the Year to Burberry.

Heading off to the Playboy party, Sienna was escorted out of the London Coliseum by bouncers, before joining other well-known faces at the Playboy Club, London, such as Poppy Delevingne.

With cocktails in hand, the pair posed for the cameras, with Poppy dressed in a cute white mini dress, adorned with grand pianos and piano keys.

Stunners: Sienna Miller and Poppy Delevingne look stunning in their dresses at the Playboy 60th anniversary party

And the award for most stylish couple goes to...: Sienna Miller and her fiancé Tom Sturridge put on a very stylish display as they made a rare red carpet appearance together at the 2013 British Fashion Awards at the London Coliseum on Monday night

Green goddess: Turning on the glamour as she posed up a storm upon her arrival at the star-studded event, the 31-year-old actress channelled her usual bohemian style in an emerald green silk plunging gown, which offered a sexy hint of side-boob

Dressed to impress: Featuring flattering ruffle detail, swathes of draped fabric and an elegant train, Sienna's choice of dress ensured she stood out from the rather stylish crowd

Featuring flattering ruffle detail, swathes of draped fabric and an elegant train, Sienna's choice of dress ensured she stood out from the rather stylish crowd.

And Tom certainly didn't let his leading lady down in the sartorial stakes, looking every inch the dapper dude in a slick two-piece suit, which he teamed with a crisp white shirt and a skinny tie.

The 27-year-old actor completed his look with smart black leather shoes and a black felt fedora as he wrapped a protective arm around the waist of his wife-to-be.

Stylish twosome: Tom certainly didn't let his leading lady down in the sartorial stakes, looking every inch the dapper dude in a slick two-piece suit, which he teamed with a crisp white shirt and a skinny tie

Adorable twosome: Sienna and Tom started dating back in 2011, before welcoming their baby daughter Marlowe into the world in July 2012

It would appear the Broadway star had been taking part in 'Movember', and was sporting a significant amount of facial furniture in the form of a perfectly-coiffed moustache as the cute couple took to the red carpet.

Sienna and Tom started dating back in 2011, before welcoming their baby daughter Marlowe into the world in July 2012.

And the pair have become key players in the fashion world since being announced as the new faces of iconic British brand Burberry earlier this year.

Natural beauty: Showing off her striking features with natural make-up, the mother-of-one wore her long  blonde locks in loose waves, and appeared to have opted for an unusual pinky hue for the occasion

Speaking about the AW'13 shoot, which was shot by Mario Testino, Burberry Chief Creative Officer Christopher Bailey said: 'This was a shoot full of love and laughter.

'It was such a treat to work with Sienna and Tom, and to capture their magic together.'

Established back in 1989, the annual British Fashion Awards has been celebrating the contributions of British designers, creatives and models to the international style scene for more than 20 years.

Fashion hit! The pair have become key players in the fashion world since being announced as the new faces of iconic British brand Burberry earlier this year

  

This year, to recognise the ever growing globalisation of the industry, the British Fashion Awards has added an International Designer of the Year category to celebrate an influential international designer making waves on the global sartorial scene.

The 2013 British Fashion Awards will see Kate Moss honoured with the Special Recognition Award for her hugely successful 25-year career and continued influence on the fashion industry as the style icon of her generation.

Early Life and Education of Montana Jordan

Montana Jordan was born on March 8, 2003, in Longview, Texas to Kelli Pieratt Smith and Tony Jordan. His mother Kelli was was a first-grade teacher. He was raised in Ore City, Texas with his two older sisters Katella Jordan and Jaden Jordan.

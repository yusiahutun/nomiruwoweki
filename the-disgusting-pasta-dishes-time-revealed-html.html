The most disgusting pasta dishes of all time revealed

 Posted on August 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  468 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Pasta is cheap, filling and can be used in a variety of different and delicious meals.

But though it's easy to cook and pairs with many ingredients, some amateur chefs manage to ruin this kitchen staple by overcooking it or serving it with some very odd items.

These photos, shared on the Reddit thread Sh***y Food Porn, are of the worst pasta dishes arguably ever made.

From congealed pasta cakes to unappetising sludge, these pictures show how even an easy-to-cook ingredient like spaghetti or penne can turn into a stomach-churning mess in the wrong hands.

And the pictures will make you feel a lot better about your own cooking skills.  

Meal prep is the social media phenomenon where gym fanatics cook up healthy meals in advance and store them in plastic containers. However this lump of congealed pasta is probably not one of the meals that is regarded as an acceptable leftover

Curry sauce, fish fingers - and pasta. While the first two ingredients might work together, the addition of pasta makes this meal an abomination

Unicorn food was one of the biggest trends of last year, and involved dyeing foods bright colours. But some meals should not be given the unicorn treatment, and pasta is one of them

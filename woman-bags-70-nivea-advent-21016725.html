Woman bags Nivea advent calendar worth 70 for just 10

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  341 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Beauty and skincare products can be very expensive.

So when deals and discounts allow customers to stock up on supplies for less, most of us jump at the chance.

This year, founder of Money Saving Expert, Martin Lewis, delighted fans when he shared a Boots hack that allowed shoppers to get £173.50 worth of beauty products for £42.

The financial guru suggested buying the No7 Advent Calendar, which retails at less than the individual products, and use the contents as gifts or supplies, pretty nifty.

And now one woman has shared a bargain with fellow shoppers on the Facebook group, Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK.

The woman explained how she'd picked up the Nivea Christmas advent calendar for just £10 at her local Morrisons over the weekend.

The Nivea advent calendar is retailing at £70 on Amazon currently, so that's a whopping £60 off.

Her incredible find racked up 5,000 reactions on Facebook over the discounted price and the contents inside.

Sharing her bargain the woman revealed the items behind the doors, which include full-size skincare products, and detailed her buy.

She said: "I know a lot of people have been posting about these Nivea advent calendars but I've yet to see a post with what's inside, so just in case anyone is curious before buying.

"£10 from Morrisons, £53 online elsewhere."

The bundle contains a mix of Nivea bestsellers, including lip balms, shower gels, moisturisers, face masks and roll on deodorant.

Shopping secrets

As well as Micellar Water, hand-cream, soap, sleep masks, hairspray, beauty sponges and more.

Commenting on the post, one Facebook user wrote: "I would get it for the lip balms alone. Such good quality."

Another added: "That's right good value."

However, the deal isn't available online, so shoppers will have to check their local Morrisons branch for available stock.

Adidas Nizza Platform Sneaker

Instantly edge up your outfits with the Adidas Nizza Platform Sneakers, which come in both sleek black and versatile white. The shoes are designed with a textile upper, 1.5-inch rubber platform, pull tab on the back for easy on-and-off, and the brand’s signature three-stripes logo on both sides. And, as one shopper pointed out, the sneakers are not only “super cute,” but also “awesomely comfortable.” 

Reebok Princess Sneaker

Every closet needs a pair of classic white sneakers, and this faux leather Reebok style is a fan favorite. Thanks to cushioned insoles, the shoes feel like “walking on clouds,” according to a reviewer. They also have anti-slip rubber outsoles to keep you stable and padded sock-liners to prevent uncomfortable skin irritation. Wear these timeless sneakers with athleisure, jeans, dresses, skirts — the list goes on. 

Dr. Scholl's Nova Slip-On Sneaker

Add a subtle pop of color to your spring wardrobe with these Dr. Scholls slip-on sneakers. They come in 16 shades, including light gray, pastel blue, and sage green, and are made from breathable microfiber with rubber outsoles. One reviewer said they “feel supportive and provide good cushioning for being on your feet all day,” while someone else deemed them “stylish and perfect in every way.”

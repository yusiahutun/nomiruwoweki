Coachella 2024: The best-dressed stars including Taylor Swift, Megan Fox, Shakira, more

 Posted on May 21, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  657 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

A-listers showed off their style credentials at the annual music festival in California

    Coachella came back with a bang on Friday – but it's not just the world-class musicians who steal the headlines, it's the jaw-dropping fashion statements too.

    Each year, the festival glam gets brighter and bolder, and there are no such things as too much glitter or too many accessories.

    From beloved Daisy dukes and sheer dresses to neon colors and this year's must-have cowboy hat, celebrities are bringing their A-game to Indio, California.

    Check out the best-dressed stars at Coachella 2024 below…

    Megan Fox

    Megan Fox channeled Western grunge at the Nylon House event and rocked a black cowboy hat with an oversized Nirvana T-shirt, loose-fit jacket, and thigh-high boots.

    She added wavy extensions to her new blue hair to "give it more Coachella energy", she told .

    Alessandra Ambrosio

    Alessandra Ambrosio looked effortlessly cool in a floor-length white dress which she wore partially unbuttoned to reveal her toned figure.

    She added a tiny pair of black shorts, belt from Streets Ahead and chunky boots, and was later seen with a black leather jacket with elaborate fringe detailing.

    Emma Roberts

    Emma Roberts was also at the CELSIUS Cosmic Desert Event and looked gorgeous in a green and white dress, which she teamed with brown cowboy boots and white-rimmed sunglasses.

    Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce

    Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce turned Coachella into another date night, both sporting contrasting yet appropriate festival fits.

    Travis opted for a white tee with flannels and striped blue pants with a bandana as a scarf, while Taylor went for black on black – a leather jacket, mini skirt, booties, and a Stella McCartney crossbody. They both brought some personality with their baseball caps, though.

    Sabrina Carpenter

    Sabrina Carpenter took to the stage in a minuscule, pleated skirt, which she teamed with a white lace top and gold jacket.

    Her rumored boyfriend, Barry Keoghan was spotted in the audience cheering her on during her set.

    Barry Keoghan

    Speaking of Barry, he attended the CELSIUS Cosmic Desert Event earlier in the day, displaying his bulging muscles in a white Versace tank top, Burberry shorts, and a matching Burberry neck wrap.

    Paris Hilton

    Paris Hilton went all black for her appearance at Neon Carnival on Saturday night after joining Vampire Weekend on stage earlier in the day.

    She wore a fringed bralette alongside a black skirt with an asymmetrical hem, adding a matching cover-up, boots, and a cowboy hat, posing for photos with friends Kyle Richards and Kesha.

    Shakira

    Shakira made a surprise appearance during Argentinian DJ Bizarrap's set in a yellow, red, and black mini-dress with matching knee-high boots that looked like colorful strings wrapped around her body.

    During her performance, she announced that she is "finally" going on tour and will kick off the Shakira, Las Mujeres Ya No Lloran World Tour, in November.

    Gwen Stefani

    Gwen Stefani rocked the Coachella stage for the long-awaited No Doubt reunion, bringing a twist to her signature flannel obsession.

    The singer wore a full cut-out tartan and plaid look, featuring a bralette with chaps, paired with peaks of skin, fishnets, and her glitzy makeup (and was joined by surprise guest Olivia Rodrigo in a "I heart ND" tee).

    Ice Spice

    For her Coachella set, Ice Spice kept things spicy with a sheer black lace catsuit that showcased her figure.

    Alongside the intricate and flattering patterns, what really made the catsuit pop was its array of cut-outs and string detailing that made it feel like it could pop off at any time during her bombastic performance.

    Nelly Furtado

    Nelly Furtado brought the Sahara Stage down on Saturday, dressed in shades of shimmering pink.

    She opted for a semi-sheer bodysuit with a sparkling sequin-covered butterfly appliqué, plus fringed gloves and wide-legged pants with tassels that moved as she danced.

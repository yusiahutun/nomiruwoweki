Kate Middleton oozes elegance in festive photo but fans all ask the same thing

 Posted on May 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  382 words  |  Martina Birk

Kate Middleton is killing the Instagram game with her latest festive snap, although many fans are asking her the same question about her piano performance at her 2021 Christmas carol concert.

The Princess of Wales poses for snaps at a “very special Carol service” on the Instagram account she shares with her husband, Prince William, and she’s definitely getting into the festive spirit.

Kate Middleton is getting into the festive spirit – but fans have one question

Taking to their 14.9 million Instagram followers, the account for the Prince and Princess of Wales posted a photo of Kate Middleton smiling against a Christmas Tree to promote their special royal carol service on Christmas Eve.

The event will be taking place at Westminster Abbey for the third year in a row. It will be available for all to watch on ITV on December 24, and “will be a special moment to thank all those who do so much to support babies, young children, and families in our communities across the UK.”

In the snap, Kate can be seen smiling next to the tree, wearing an elegant white turtleneck jumper, with festive patterns.

Many took to the comments to reveal how excited they were, although others just wanted to know if she was going to play the piano again.

“Hope she plays the piano again!!” exclaimed one, while another asked: “Would you be playing the piano? Absolutely Love How Talented You Are.”

In 2021, for Kate’s first Christmas Carol performance, she played the instrument, accompanying Tom Walker in his performance of For Those Who Can’t Be Here, who said she “absolutely nailed it.”

The Princess stuns in all-red outfit

Although this one may not be for festive reasons, Kate was seen stunning in an all-red outfit as she and William welcomed the President of South Korea, Yoon Suk Yeol, and his wife to the UK for their two-day visit.

In her flag-dressing tradition, Kate tends to wear the colors of the country she is visiting or hosting. She stunned in a red cape and red hat, although also cleverly gave a nod to the blue in Korea’s flag with her accessories.

The 41-year-old opted for a pair of sapphire and diamond drop earrings which belonged to her late mother-in-law, Princess Diana.

EastEnders' Kim Medcalf wows fans with glam new look

 Posted on April 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  394 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

EastEnders fans are gushing over Kim Medcalf's glam new look after she returned to the soap as Sam Mitchell this week.

Kim's return comes 17 years after she last appeared as Phil Mitchell's younger sister in a stint spanning from 2003 to 2005.

More recently, fans of the BBC soap have been used to seeing original actress Danniella Westbrook in the role.

She played Sam between 1990 and 2000, and reprised the role for guest stints in 2009, 2010 and 2016.

Fortunately, avid EastEnders viewers appear to be thrilled by Kim's return, with many sharing their praise for the character's new look on Twitter.

In light of Sam's dramatic return to Walford, fans have been debating which actress best portrayed her - Kim or Danniella.

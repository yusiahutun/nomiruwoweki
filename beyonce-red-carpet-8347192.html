20 Years of Beyonc on the Red Carpet

 Posted on April 9, 2024  |  12 minutes  |  2467 words  |  Aldo Pusey

After starting out as part of the chart-topping trio Destiny's Child, Beyoncé Knowles-Carter has evolved from an emerging starlet to a global icon. From the days when the group donned matching outfits on the red carpet to all her looks throughout a solo career that’s taken the world by storm, Beyoncé's style has transformed and matured in parallel with her growth as an artist.

For over two decades, Queen Bey has given us unforgettable fashion moments. She’s a triple threat and she’s also brought a diverse array of style choices to her red-carpet appearances. Whether she's debuting her baby bump in a daring red sequin gown by Peter Dundas at the Grammys, turning heads in a “naked dress” by Givenchy at the Met Gala, or channeling Old Hollywood glam in a black-velvet Atelier Versace number at her Oscars debut in 2005, Beyoncé's multifaceted red-carpet style never fails to leave us speechless.

Join us as we revisit 20 years of Beyoncé's best red carpet moments.

Beyoncé in Gucci at the 2023 Grammys

At the 2023 Grammy Awards, after winning "Best Dance/Electronic Music Album" for Renaissance, Beyoncé established a new record with 32 Grammy wins. To accept her record-breaking trophy, she donned a custom metallic Gucci gown paired with black leather gloves.

Beyoncé Attends the Atlantis Royal Hotel Grand Reveal

After a 5-year hiatus, Beyoncé made a triumphant return to the stage, performing at the grand unveiling of The Atlantis Royal Hotel in Dubai. The superstar reportedly earned a staggering $24 million for her appearance, and she looked like 24 million bucks in this bold gold ensemble.

Beyoncé in a Black Minidress at the 2022 Grammys

Beyoncé skipped the carpet at the 2022 Grammys, but we got a peak at her outfit when she took the stage alongside Megan Thee Stallion to accept the award for Best Rap Performance. She looked stunning in a black leather minidress by Schiaparelli with leather gloves and bold earrings.

Beyoncé at the European Premiere of 'The Lion King'

At the European premiere of The Lion King, Beyoncé made a stunning entrance on the red carpet in a radiant gold dress by Cong Tri. The focal point was the daring slit that showed off her metallic shoes.

Beyoncé at the LA Premiere of 'The Lion King'

At the Los Angeles premiere of The Lion King, Beyoncé stunned in an Alexander McQueen tuxedo dress adorned with crystals. She accessorized with shimmering jewels, matching crystal-encrusted heels, and a dramatic clutch.

Beyoncé in Red Sequins at the 2017 Grammys

Glowing with her growing baby bump, Beyoncé graced the red carpet in a sequined red gown by Peter Dundas and a dazzling diamond necklace. With her Grammy awards in hand, she celebrated her wins for the groundbreaking Lemonade album and its leading single, "Formation."

Beyoncé Attends the Tidal Philanthropic Event in 2016

At Tidal's philanthropic event in 2016, Beyoncé stole the show in an intricately beaded sheer black dress. She enhanced the ensemble with stacks of bangles on each wrist and wore her hair in a sleek braided ponytail, drawing attention to her striking statement earrings.

Beyoncé in a Sheer Beaded Gown at the 2016 VMAs

At the 2016 VMAs, Beyoncé was the show’s most celebrated artist. Her visual album Lemonade garnered the top honor, Video of the Year, for "Formation." Additionally, she took home awards for Best Female Video, Best Pop Video, and Breakthrough Long Form Video, among others. The most touching part? Walking hand-in-hand with daughter Blue Ivy while wearing a feathered Francesco Scognamiglio gown.

Beyoncé Attends the 2016 Met Gala in Latex Givenchy

At the 2016 Met Gala, themed "Manus x Machina: Fashion in an Age of Technology," Beyoncé turned heads in a latex Givenchy gown. The skin-tight dress in a soft peach hue was beaded with floral and pearl embellishments.

Beyoncé in a Sheer Gown at the 2015 Met Gala

At the 2015 Met Gala, Beyoncé turned heads in a sheer Givenchy gown adorned with multicolored crystals and sequins that left little to the imagination. The theme of the night was "China: Through the Looking Glass.” Beyoncé's daring look made her one of the most talked-about attendees of the night. Her high ponytail and flawless makeup added the finishing touches to this show-stopping appearance.

Beyoncé Attends the 2015 Grammys in Proenza Schouler

There's a common theme here: Beyoncé loves an embellished black dress. At the 2015 Grammys, she exuded sophistication in a tailored Proenza Schouler black lace gown.

Beyoncé in the 2015 Grammys Press Room

Beyoncé proudly showcased her awards for "Best R&B Performance," "Best R&B Song," and "Best Surround Sound Album” in a beaded little black dress she complemented with standout sandals.

Beyoncé Attends the 2014 Met Gala in Givenchy

At the 2014 Met Gala, celebrating the "Charles James: Beyond Fashion" theme, Beyoncé made a memorable entrance. She donned a sheer black Givenchy gown adorned with intricate beading. The ensemble exuded a sense of opulence and modernity, aligning perfectly with the night's homage to couture and elegance.

Beyoncé in Michael Costello at the 2014 Grammys

At the 2014 Grammys, Beyoncé graced the press room in a white, sheer dress designed by Michael Costello. Earlier that evening, she set the stage alight, opening the show with a performance of "Drunk in Love" alongside her husband, Jay-Z.

Beyoncé's Tom Ford Gown at the Screening of Her Album

At the release party and screening for her self-titled album, Beyoncé, Ms. Knowles dazzled in a high-neck, long-sleeve sheer black dress from Tom Ford's Spring 2014 collection. She paired it with matching boots and intensified the look with a smokey eye.

Beyoncé at the 2013 Met Gala in Riccardo Tisci for Givenchy

As an honorary chair for the 2013 Met Gala, themed “Punk: Chaos to Couture,” Beyoncé's ensemble was truly unforgettable. Designed by Riccardo Tisci for Givenchy, Her gown featured a skirt with a high slit, embellished with a striking orange flame design.

Beyoncé at the 2013 Grammys in a Black and White Jumpsuit

At the 2013 Grammy Awards, Beyoncé caught us off guard with a departure from her usual high-octane glamor. She went for a chic graphic jumpsuit from Osman Studio, complementing it with a sleek low ponytail, a bold red lip, understated earrings, and Art Deco bangles.

Beyoncé in a Black Sheer Gown at the 2012 Met Gala

At the 2012 Met Gala, themed “Schiaparelli and Prada: Impossible Conversations,” Beyoncé turned heads in a sheer Givenchy couture creation adorned with crystal accents, intricate black lace, and a gradient feathered train.

Beyoncé in a One-Shoulder Lanvin Gown at the 2011 VMAs

At the 2011 VMAs, Beyoncé stylishly debuted her baby bump. She was radiant in a one-shoulder Lanvin gown.

Beyoncé in Black at the 2011 Billboard Music Awards

This look screams 2011. The long-sleeve minidress paired with the peep-toe Christian Louboutin pumps? We love.

Beyoncé at the 2011 Met Gala in Emilio Pucci

At the 2011 Met Gala, themed “Alexander McQueen: Savage Beauty,” Beyoncé captivated attendees in a black-and-gold Emilio Pucci gown. The dress, with its high neckline, embroidered bodice, and tulle skirt, was truly show-stopping.

Beyoncé at the 'I Am… World Tour' Screening

Beyoncé made an entrance at a New York City screening of her concert film, I Am… World Tour that was true to her signature style. She wore a beaded minidress by Naeem Khan, complemented by Lorraine Schwartz jewels and Christian Louboutin shoes.

Beyoncé at the 2010 Grammys in Stéphane Rolland

At the 2010 Grammy Awards, Beyoncé graced the event in a blush Stéphane Rolland gown, its exposed zipper detail lending an edgy flair. She complemented the ensemble with bold gold jewelry and shimmering strands of hair tinsel.

Beyoncé in Atelier Versace at the 2009 EMAs

The singer won three awards at the 2009 EMAs and captivated the audience with her performance of “Sweet Dreams.” Equally mesmerizing on the red carpet, she looked like a sweet dream in an Atelier Versace gown.

Beyoncé Attends the 2009 Oscars in House of Deréon

At the 2009 Academy Awards, Beyoncé struck a pose in a striking black-and-gold gown from House of Deréon, a brand helmed by her mother, Tina Knowles.

Beyoncé in Roberto Cavalli at the 2009 VMAs

Ah, the “Single Ladies” era! Beyoncé performed her smash hit at the 2009 VMAs. Posing with her awards, the singer looked ravishing in a red Roberto Cavalli minidress.

Beyoncé at the 2009 BET Awards in Balmain

Bey’s look for the 2009 BET Awards was everything. The singer donned a sparkly Balmain minidress with chandelier style sandals and bold green earrings.

Beyoncé Attends the Obsessed Premiere in 2009

The Obsessed star showed off her fabulous figure at the premiere in a sequin Balmain minidress that featured a dramatic train—we’re obsessed!

Beyoncé in Elie Saab at the 2009 Golden Globes

At the 2009 Golden Globes, Beyoncé embodied Hollywood royalty. She opted for a champagne gown by one of her go-to designers, Elie Saab. Yet it was the dazzling diamond necklace that truly took this look to the next level.

Beyoncé in a Silver and Blue Gown at the 2008 Grammys

Queen B had a true princess moment at the 2008 Grammys. Beyoncé channeled Cinderella vibes on the red carpet, donning a blue tulle Elie Saab gown featuring a structured sequined bodice complemented by matching jewelry.

Beyoncé in Yellow at the 2008 Grammys Afterparty

Beyoncé was a ray of sunshine at the 2008 Grammy Awards Sony BMG Afterparty in a yellow Stéphane Rolland gown and Lorraine Schwartz earrings.

Beyoncé in Armani Privé at the 2008 Met Gala

Beyoncé posed with Giorgio Armani in an Armani Privé strapless blush pink gown for her first-ever Met Gala in 2008. The theme was “Superheroes: Fashion and Fantasy.”

Beyoncé in Green Lamé at the 2007 VMAs

Draped in a Grecian-style lamé dress, Beyoncé radiated golden-goddess vibes. She accessorized with piles of bangles, and her high ponytail highlighted chic chandelier earrings. That year, she and Shakira won the Best Collaboration award for their iconic song, "Beautiful Liar.”

Beyoncé at the 2007 Oscars in Armani Privé

The Dreamgirls star looked radiant in a seafoam green Armani Privé gown. 

Beyoncé in Giorgio Armani at the 2017 Grammys

Taking a brief detour from her usual opulent beaded gowns, Beyoncé opted for a chic silk minidress by Giorgio Armani. That year, she performed "Listen" from Dreamgirls.

Beyoncé Attends the 'Dreamgirls' UK Premiere in 2007

Beyoncé Knowles, who portrayed Deena Jones—a character inspired by Motown star and lead Supremes member Diana Ross—in Dreamgirls, looked dreamy in a beaded Elie Saab gown at the film's London premiere.

Beyoncé at the 2007 Golden Globes in Elie Saab

Knowles resembled a Golden Globe trophy in this breathtaking beaded Elie Saab dress, which was perfectly complemented by her metallic makeup.

Beyoncé in a Blue Gown at the 2006 AMAs

Bold in blue at the 2006 American Music Awards, the singer won Favorite Soul/R&B Female Artist that evening. 

Beyoncé in Atelier Versace at the MTV VMAs in 2006

Following her unforgettable "Ring The Alarm" performance at the 2006 VMAs, Beyoncé stunned in Atelier Versace as she posed with her award for "Best R&B Video.”

Beyoncé Attends the Pink Panther Premiere in 2006

For the Pink Panther premiere in New York City, it was only fitting for the actress to dazzle in pink. She looked stunning in a satin Givenchy dress, accentuated by a sheer overlay embellished with intricate floral details.

Beyoncé At the Dreamgirls New York City Premiere in 2006

The songstress appeared along with her co-stars, Jennifer Hudson and Anika Noni Rose, at the Ziegfeld Theatre for the New York City premiere of Dreamgirls.

Beyoncé Attends the 2005 VMAs in a Silver Gown

Beyoncé, Kelly Rowland, and Michelle Williams attended the VMAs together even though Beyoncé had already launched her solo career. The trio was nominated for best dance video for "Lose My Breath" and best group video for "Soldier." These singles hailed from Destiny's Child's fourth and final album, Destiny Fulfilled.

Beyoncé at the BET Awards in 2005

Destiny’s Child was no stranger to coordinated looks on the red carpet. At the 2005 BET Awards, where they won the "Best Group" award, they dazzled in an array of lavender hues.

Beyoncé at the 2005 Oscars in Black Velvet

Making her Oscars debut in 2005, the singer graced the red carpet in a stunning black velvet Atelier Versace gown complemented by shimmering diamond earrings.

Beyoncé Attends the Vanity Fair Oscars Afterparty in 2005

Bey attended the Vanity Fair Oscars afterparty in a baby blue Roberto Cavalli gown. Her pulled-back tresses put some gorgeous diamond earrings on display.

Beyoncé at the 2005 Grammys in Roberto Cavalli

Beyoncé looked radiant in a Roberto Cavalli black dress with gold details at the Grammys in 2005. The best part? She brought her dad, Matthew Knowles, as her date.

Beyoncé and Jay-Z’s Red Carpet Debut at the 2004 VMAs

It was all about couples coordination for Beyoncé and Jay-Z’s red carpet debut at the 2004 MTV VMAs. Bey’s gold short suit matched Jay’s tie. 

Beyoncé at the 2004 Grammys

Beyoncé attended the Grammys solo for the first time in 2004. Here she is posing with all five of her awards in a golden gown. 

Beyoncé Attends the 2003 Billboard Music Awards

Beyoncé attended the 2003 Billboard music awards with her mother, Tina Knowles, by her side. 

Beyoncé at the 2003 EMAs

Beyoncé dazzled in a revealing crystal look at the 2003 EMAs. 

Beyoncé at the 2003 Essence Awards

Bey stepped out in a rare gothic moment at the Essence Awards. With the lace and corseted elements and what appears to be a giant Chrome Hearts dagger necklace, she was really in her edgy-girl era. 

Beyoncé at the 2003 MTV Movie Awards in Versace

Just like Beyoncé, this green corseted Versace dress from the Fall 2003 collection is an icon.

Tex-Mex Chicken Meatballs Recipe

 Posted on July 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  229 words  |  Martina Birk
Ian Palmer

Looking for an easy Tex-Mex meal? Try making these delicious ground chicken meatballs, which are packed with flavor from spices and mild Hatch chiles. You can make an excellent light taco salad-style dinner as we suggest here, or serve them as an appetizer with guacamole and/or salsa for dipping, or as a side dish for a larger Tex-Mex feast! Pair 'em with Pink Grapefruit Margaritas for a true celebration.

Yields: 4 - 6 serving(s)

Prep Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 30 mins

Ingredients

Save Recipe

  • Cooking spray

  • 1 lb.

    ground chicken

  • 1

    large egg, beaten

  • 1/4 c.

    plain breadcrumbs

  • 1/3 c.

     chopped onion

  • 1 1/2 tsp.

    chopped garlic

  • 1

    (4-ounce) can chopped Hatch chiles

  • 1/2 tsp.

    chili powder

  • 1/2 tsp.

    ground cumin

  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper

  • 1

    head romaine lettuce, roughly chopped

  • Guacamole, salsa, and grated cheese, for serving

Directions

  • Step 1Preheat oven to 400°F. Coat a small sheet pan with cooking spray. Combine chicken, egg, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, Hatch chiles, chili powder, and cumin in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Form mixture into 9 (about 2 ounces each) meatballs, and place on prepared pan. Lightly grease meatballs with cooking spray. Bake until cooked through, 18 to 20 minutes.
  • Step 2 Serve meatballs on romaine topped with guacamole, salsa, and cheese.

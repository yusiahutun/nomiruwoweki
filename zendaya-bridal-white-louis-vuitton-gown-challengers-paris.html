Zendaya wears bridal-inspired Louis Vuitton gown

 Posted on March 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  323 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Zendaya has had a busy weekend with back-to-back engagements for the Paris premiere of her new sporty romance film Challengers, which means we got to enjoy several masterful tennis-themed looks. While her first couple of outfits were full of tennis ball-green hues, she went a subtler route for her last.

The actress seemed to have embodied the Wimbledon dress code (which requires competitors to wear tennis attire that is almost entirely white) in an ethereal white gown.

Marc Piasecki

The custom Louis Vuitton design featured a strapless corset bodice with a sweetheart neckline and a sweeping, slightly-sheer skirt that was composed of flowy, gauzy fabric with pleats throughout and a lettuce hem.

Zendaya, with the help of her stylist Law Roach, added a wide white leather belt that criss-crossed at the centre for an edgy touch. To up the ante even more, she wore a beautiful diamond necklace with a centre pendant, classic diamond stud earrings, and several diamond rings, all by Bulgari. As for beauty, Zendaya glammed up with rosy make-up, complete with a bronzy smokey eye and glossy lips, and styled her newly debuted caramel-blonde hair down behind her with a side part.

EMMANUEL DUNAND//Getty Images

The angelic look capped off a day of outfit changes. Hours later, she made another statement in a shiny satin highlighter-green co-ord composed of a button-down shirt with a mandarin collar, which she wore mostly open by only fastening one button, and matching low-waisted pants. For this look, she added metallic chrome platform heels with decorative straps going up the front of her foot and a small structured purse in the same reflective green shade.

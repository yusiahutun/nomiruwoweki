Arrokuda is a Water-type Pokémon.

Arrokuda is a fierce Water-type Pokémon known for its sharp fins and powerful swimming abilities. It belongs to the Galar region and is often found in lakes and rivers.

Arrokuda evolves into Barraskewda.

Vera Sidika Blasts Critics Claiming She Denied Guests Food During Baby Shower: &quot;Mmezoea Cheap Partie

 Posted on February 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  386 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • Vera Sidika's star-studded baby shower took place on Sunday, October 10, in an extravagant set up
  • After the event, Vera was accused of posting an old food photo to lie that she had served her guests some appetising dishes which was allegedly untrue
  • The heavily pregnant socialite responded to those trolls saying that food is usually served in a different setup away from the décor at high end parties

Vera Sidika has angrily responded to trolls claiming that she denied her guests food during her baby shower.

Vera's lavish baby shower

The much-hyped baby shower went down on Sunday, October 10, at a posh residential place in one of Nairobi's affluent neighbourhoods.

From the decor to the seats, pink colour dominated the lavish baby shower.

The heavily pregnant Vera dressed in a long pink custom-made dress, while Brown Mauzo, the father of her unborn kid wore a white suit and a pink pocket square.

A day later, reports emerged that the socialite lifted an old food photo to claim that she had served her guests some high class buffet from some high-end restaurant.

Responding to the claims

Taking to Instagram stories, Vera shared screenshots of the allegations plus pictures of the food and drinks to dispel such claims.

She schooled the critics that in high-end parties like the baby shower, food are kept away from the main scene.

She blasted them that they are used to ordinary events where everything is done at the main setup.

"Haters will be in their single room hating on a party they didn’t attend. How are you not at a party and claim there was no food or drink and how is that your business anyway? " she posed."The problem with broke people is that they don't understand that in high end parties food and buffet are done away from the decor setup. They are used to cheap parties. They also don't know that plates for decor in most cases are not necessarily used as serving plates. They are for decor and should stay untouched. Some of these haters are used to going to party on empty stomach only to serve five plates of food unlike rich people," read another post.

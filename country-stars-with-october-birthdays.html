See Which Country Stars Have October Birthdays

 Posted on February 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  231 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

It's fall, y'all! Now that it's October fall is finally underway, and Halloween is just around the corner. It's not just a month full of football, trick or treating and boots — it's also the same month a lot of country stars will celebrate their births.

This month there are a lot of country stars that will be blowing out their birthday candles. The very-much-missed Chris LeDoux is a birthday we will celebrate, while also missing his talents and music. He would have celebrated on Oct. 2. The Cadillac Three’s Jaren Johnston was born on Oct. 4, 1980, and we'll definitely have a cold one for the talented (and hilarious) singer-songwriter.

We know McCreerians will celebrate their favorite singer, Scotty McCreery, on Oct. 9. Tanya Tucker will blow out her October candles the following day. Shane McAnally may not be a singer, but he's one of the best songwriters in Nashville. We'll celebrate his way with words on Oct. 12. Thomas Rhett will probably do something special the following day since his dad, Rhett Akins, celebrates his day.

Kimberly Schlapman, Alan Jackson, Josh Gracin, Jimi Westbrook and Dwight Yoakam also will celebrate. Minnie Pearl, Charlie Daniels, Keith Urban and Brad Paisley are just a few more of the country singers who have birthdays this month. You'll have to click through the gallery above to see who else we'll raise a glass to this October. Cheers!

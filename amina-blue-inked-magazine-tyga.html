Amina Blue Goes Topless In Inked Magazine -- Meet the Model Who Spent the Weekend with Tyga!

 Posted on July 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  458 words  |  Aldo Pusey

This tatted up stunner made headlines over the weekend after Tyga shared a scantily-clad photo of her on his Instagram page.

The rapper, who recently split up from Kylie Jenner, simply captioned a pic of model Amina Blue "1 of 1," as the two kicked back in Jamaica. It's unclear if she's simply appearing in a new music video with Jenner's ex or if there's something more going on there ... but everyone's talking about her.

While we wait for more details on their relationship, we got our hands on Blue's new Inked Magazine cover and sizzling spread inside, where she opens up about her celebrity look-alikes and tattoo-covered skin.

Amina's already got a past with the Kardashian-Jenner clan, as she worked in Kanye West's Yeezy shows. She's appeared in CR Fashion Book -- which has featured Kim on numerous occasions -- and, coming in at 5-foot-one and rocking an impressive derriere, she's also got some of those Kardashian proportions.

In the magazine, she reveals she's been compared to Angelina Jolie, Jessie J and Lionel Richie's model daughter, Sofia Richie.

But her trademark: all that body art!

"Besides my eyes and body shape, I think the most signature part of my look would be the Eye of Horus tattoo on my chest," she tells the mag. "I got the tattoobecause I felt as if many people around me gave me sort of 'the evil eye' or bad luck and this was my way to keep those bad spirits away from me. It has a very special meaning to me and I definitely think it's something that enhances my look."

Her other pieces include mermaids -- including Disney's Ariel -- along her thigh and a tribute to her late brother on her ribs.

Inked Magazine

"I do think tattoos are being accepted more by high fashion, surely, but slowly," she continues. "I've been blessed to add to that. I'm one of the few—if any—models to have sleeves of ink and be featured in many high fashion photoshoots and campaigns. I would like to add to one of my tattoos and make it a bit bolder. I am very proud of the way I look. I work hard to maintain my image."

And, like any good star building her brand these days, she's got a shoe line of her own in the works ... with a unique twist.

"Currently I am focused on the release of my women's vegan shoe line," she explains. "I'm extremely excited to bring veganism and fashion together, creating amazing heels that women can wear without causing any harm to animals!"

No word yet on when we can expect to see the line, but consider us intrigued!

Amina's issue of Inked Magazine hits newsstands tomorrow.

Catfish &amp; The Bottlemen Plot North American Tour

 Posted on February 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  271 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

CARDIFF, Wales (CelebrityAccess) — Welsh indie-rock quarter Catfish & The Bottlemen announced plans for a tour of North America this spring.

The tour kicks off on March 12th at the Egyptian Room at Old National Centre in Indianapolis and runs through early May when the group performs at Brooklyn Steel in New York City.

As well, the band will be doing a nearly sold-out arena run in the UK, starting on February 19th at the Motorpoint Arena in Nottingham, with shows scheduled through the rest of the month.

While the band has not released a studio album since 2016’s well-received The Ride, which peaked at #3 on the US Rock Charts, they just dropped a new music video, Longshot, which should provide some comfort to their long-suffering fans.

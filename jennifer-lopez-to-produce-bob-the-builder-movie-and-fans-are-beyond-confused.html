Jennifer Lopez to produce Bob The Builder movie and fans are beyond confused

 Posted on May 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  397 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

In a 2024 plot twist, it’s been announced Jennifer Lopez is producing a Bob The Builder animated movie at Mattel. Could we be seeing Barbie part two on our hands?

The music star is an all-rounder, a highly acclaimed singer, actress, and now producer. With Margot Robbie and Greta Gerwig taking over the world with Barbie, we’re sure Jennifer Lopez will be determined to ‘mirror’ the success. And it looks like she may have hired the right man for the role…

Jennifer Lopez set to produce Bob The Builder movie – but with a twist

The movie already has an A-List team working behind it, with J Lo as a producer, and Transformers star Anthony Ramos set to be behind the voice of everyone’s favorite builder.

Adding a twist to the much-loved TV series, the Mattel movie will see Bob travel to Puerto Rico for a major construction job. The feature film will see Bob “take on issues affecting the island and dig deeper into what it means to build.”

The official line states the children’s character will “celebrate the vibrant and colorful textures of the Caribbean Latin nations and their people.” 

Ramos will also be named as a producer in the film, alongside Alex Bulkley and Corey Campodonico of the animation and production studio ShadowMachine. Jennifer Lopez, Elaine Goldsmith-Thomas, and Benny Medina also have joined to co-produce for Nuyorican Productions.

A director has not yet been announced, however, Felipe Vargas is attached to write the screenplay.

The announcement comes after the Jenny From The Block singer was nominated for a Razzie Award for her performance in Netflix‘s The Mother.

Fans can’t get over the ‘random’ project

It definitely came as a shock to fans seeing JLo and Bob The Builder together. It’s definitely something different to what she’d usually do, to say the least.

The news may have been too much for fans to take in, as they’re still recovering from Greta Gerwig and Margot Robbie’s Oscar ‘snub’. Some, however, can’t wait to see how it turns out.

“Why is J Lo producing a ‘Bob the Builder’ movie? I’m curious to see how it turns out!” questioned one.

Another tweeted: “This is so random [cry emoji].”

“She’s too hot for this,” a third joked.

One excited fan exclaimed: “That sounds like an exciting project!”

Can J Lo fix it? Yes she can!

18 Khaki Pants Outfit Ideas to Upgrade Your Wardrobe

 Posted on June 5, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  868 words  |  Aldo Pusey

First, it was cardigans. Then, it was polo shirts. Now, khakis have made their way into our wardrobes once again. These beige, sometimes olive or light brown bottoms were a staple in the '90s and early '00s — celebrities were often seen wearing low-rise versions with shrunken graphic tees and chain belts (the ultimate throwback look). However, today, we're suddenly stumped. We know there's a way to make these pants feel modern, cool, and less dad-playing-golf. We just need a little inspiration before putting together our outfit.

Thankfully, there are plenty of ideas out there for what to wear with khakis, and we've since learned to treat them like our favorite pair of jeans. Crop tops, sweatshirts, and button-downs are all acceptable options, and you can even switch things up by pairing them with denim or leather. With a few quick tricks, these classically preppy pants can turn into a full statement-making look and might just become the most versatile thing you own in your closet.

Keep scrolling for 18 khaki pants outfits that you'll want to wear on repeat.

Keep It Consistent

When in doubt, stick with a scheme, styling your khakis with pieces from the same beige and brown color family. It will instantly look elevated.

Bring on the Basics

Fully embrace preppy style by wearing your pants with a sweater, loafers, and a bold belt. In case this outfit gives off excess grandpa vibes, consider a cropped sweater or a bracket instead. Also, keep in mind: Grandparents are today's fashion muses.

Add a Bit of Camo

This can be in the form of a jacket, a button-down, or a T-shirt. The combination of colors always works.

Get Comfy

With the help of an oversized hoodie, layered turtleneck, and a pair of sneakers, khakis will seem more casual and laid-back. It's the loungewear spin-off that we can get behind.

Switch Things Up with Leather

A leather jacket or button-down isn't something you'd expect to see with these basic bottoms — which is why you should try it. Not to mention, it will take you from the office to after-work drinks seamlessly, making it the perfect day-to-night look.

Try Denim

When in need of style inspiration, look no further than Bella Hadid. Here, the contrasting colors, cuts, and materials of her khaki-denim ensemble are what makes it especially eye-catching (okay, that and her bare midriff, too).

Create a Sexy Spin

Turn up the heat ahead of a night out by opting for a sheer top to go along with your khaki pants. To layer this look, throw on an oversized blazer with '80s-inspired shoulder pads — or not, it's up to you.

Pull on Some Boots

Not only is the shoe choice great for colder days, but tucking baggy pants into knee-high boots is a model-approved move. It's also visual proof that everyone's dressing like ice cream cones right now.

Throw on a Vest

Yes, vests are back, too. A puff-sleeved shirt underneath will make this outfit seem less stuffy, and cropped khakis will serve as its neutral base.

Remember the Classics

The good news is that khakis pair well with your other wardrobe staples, like white tees, blazers, and Converse sneakers. The result? You have a foolproof outfit formula that you can count on, especially when you're short on time and out of ideas.

Experiment with Different Styles

Not all khakis are alike. Some are cropped, have pockets, or feature wider legs, so it's worth finding a cut that suits your specific style.

Complete with a Crop Top

If your pants are a higher rise, a crop top will balance out proportions and keep your silhouette from feeling bulky. Pair with heels for an elegant finish.

Go with a Button-Down

It's a tried-and-true ensemble. Both long- and short-sleeved options will help make these pants feel polished, especially if they're naturally casual cargos.

Accent with Metallics

TBH, we never thought to mix metallics and khakis together. Here, Gigi Hadid proves that the combo is unexpected, daring, and therefore, highly encouraged.

Add a Pop of Color

If you really want to stand out, reach for something red, which will always make a statement when styled with beige bottoms. The vibrant hue is also the color of the season.

Break Out the Leopard Print

This animal print is similar in color and will blend nicely with khakis, while also adding an element of fun. Let's call it urban safari.

Let Loose

For summer days, a lighter pair of pants will come across as effortless when paired with a long, oversized button-down or a shirtdress. Heck, even a regular dress would work. After all, if celebrities are embracing the dress-over-pants trend, so can you.

Style with a Tank

When it comes to warmer weather, don't overthink — cropped khakis and a solid-colored (or even striped) tank top are already a full easy, breezy outfit. Finish with loafers or the footwear of your choice.

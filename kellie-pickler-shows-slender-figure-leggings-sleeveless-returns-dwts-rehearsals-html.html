After weeks of gruelling rehearsals and nerve racking live performances it's no surprise that country singer Kellie Pickler is in amazing shape.

The 26-year-old country singer showed off her toned arms and shapely legs as she left rehearsals for Dancing with the Stars on Wednesday.

Kellie looked in good spirits in a loose fitting sleeveless top over clinging black leggings and flip flops.

Sleek and toned: Kellie Pickler showed off her stunning dancer's figure as she left rehearsals in Los Angels on Thursday

She checked her cell phone before getting into into her white BMW and putting the top down to feel the relaxing breeze while she drove home after an extended rehearsal.

She was followed out by her dance partner Derek Hough who sported a black vest top that showed off his ripped arms, along with black pants and shoes.

The professional dancer, who has won the competition three times in the past, carried a laptop and speakers out of the rehearsal space.

End of a long day: The country music star didn't even change before hopping into her BMW to leave

Pretty Pickler: Country musician Kelly Pickler leaves put the top down on her BMW convertible for the drive home

Doing well: The star is leading the way in the competition, reaching top spot on the leader board on Monday night

The pair were back practising after a successful week where they reached top of the leader board, meaning they had immunity from elimination. 

The seventh week in the series was full of drama and emotion.

It featured ballroom dancer Adrian Haslet who had lost her leg in the Boston terrorism attack vowing to dance again, as well as the tearful elimination of fan favourite Andy Dick and his professional partner Sharna Burgess.

Looking buff: DWTS dancer Derek Hough strolls through the parking lot after a tough rehearsal with a laptop and speakers

Amazing shape: Hough displayed his cut arms in a sleeveless black muscle shirt after spending the day in rehearsals

U.S. Olympic gymnast Alexandra Raisman and two-time DWTS champ Mark Ballas were also hard at work on Wednesday, trying to make up ground. 

In the middle of their rehearsals, the pair took a walk outside together .

While Raisman and Ballas had the highest score in week eight, they're still very much in contention for the Mirror Ball if they have a few more winning weeks.

Talking strategy: U.S. Olympic gymnast Alexandra Raisman and two-time DWTS champ Mark Ballas take a walk mid-rehearsal for a break

Perhaps losing his first place position put Val Chemerkovskiy in a serious mood upon his arrival.

Wearing a tight long sleeve white shirt, tan rolled-up corduroys and white high top Converse, the usually cheery dancer headed inside the studio.

Meanwhile, 16-year-old teen star Zendaya, who is partnered with Chemerkovskiy, seemed to be her usual bubbly self, sporting a black flowery top and grey leggings.

Staying serious: After losing first place to Hough and Pickler, Val Chemerkovskiy was in a serious mood upon arriving at rehearsals Wednesday

Teenage enthusiasm: Chemerkovskiy's partner 16-year-old teen star Zendaya was her usual bubbly self

Meanwhile, the hulky Baltimore Ravens wide receiver and partner Kym Johnson were placed in jeopardy after last night's low scored performance.

The 28-year-old NFL star fell into the bottom three, but has not yet been eliminated.

Bachelor star Sean Lowe was also given low scores on the evening, but managed to survive to dance another day with partner Petra Murgatroyd.

An inspiration: Boston bombing survivor Adrian Haslet lost a leg, but vows to return to her professional ballroom dancing career

A disappointment: Fan favourite Andy Dick was eliminated on the Week 7 episode

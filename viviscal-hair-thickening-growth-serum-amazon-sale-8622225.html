Despite having differing textures, lengths, and tones, most of us aspire for hair that’s shiny, healthy, and full — key indicators of healthy tresses. Fortunately, shine-inducing sprays, thickening lotions, and hair-growth serums exist, though not every product offers all three benefits, as Viviscal’s Thickening Serum does. 

The Viviscal Thickening Serum is a leave-in hair treatment that envelops strands in a densifying, lightweight formula. Hailing from a brand that specializes in growth-promoting supplements and follicle-enhancing topicals, Viviscal’s lotion-like styling cream plumps thinning, lackluster hair in seconds. Over time, it strengthens feeble strands, smooths frizz without leaving the greasy residue inherent to many strand-smoothing products, and imparts lustrous shine to give hair a healthier appearance overall. Best of all, the multi-tasker is on sale at Amazon, where it’s $14 right now.

The serum-like leave-in treatment is straightforward to use, per shoppers, and works on wet or dry hair, making it versatile and a breeze to incorporate into any existing styling routine. Moreover, it’s ideal for all hair types and textures and safe for color- and keratin-treated hair.

Key ingredients include biotin, a form of vitamin B, and keratin, the structural protein that functions as a building block for nails and hair. Biotin, when taken in supplement-form, may improve hair strength and promote new growth; as a topical ingredient, it adds moisture and shine. Keratin functions as a strengthening ingredient when used topically, hence its prevalence in fortifying hair products and inclusion in Viviscal’s serum. 

Shoppers are impressed by the formula — both for its instant and long-term benefits. Similarly, another reviewer says the serum “provides weightless volume.” Yet another fan swears their hair was “thicker [and] fuller” after using the product for only a week; after one month, it looks “20 percent fuller,” they claim. Finally, a shopper whose hair is naturally “very fine” says Viviscal’s serum imparts thickness and tames “flyaway ends.”

How to Dress Like a Leo, According to an Expert Astrologist

 Posted on September 9, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  720 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

When it comes to the zodiac, fire signs stand out as fashion stars, and Leos are no exception. With Leo season hot on our tails — it officially runs from July 23 through August 22 — there's no better time to embrace Leo style, but don't just take our word for it. To really dive into this sign's fiery fashion, InStyle tapped Bri Luna, founder of TheHoodwitch and author of the forthcoming Blood, Sex Magic, to give us all the deets on Leo clothing style.

"Most Leos relate to fashion as a means of self-expression and personal empowerment," says Luna. "They are not afraid to make bold fashion choices and stand out from the rest of the crowd."

Luna explains that Leos are ruled by the Sun, which symbolizes power, vitality, and self-expression. Thanks to their planetary ruler, Leos are known for being confident and charismatic, two traits that influence their spotlight-stealing approach to fashion. "They often gravitate towards vibrant and luxurious garments that reflect their confidence they love attention-grabbing outfits that make a statement and showcase their individuality," Luna says, a fact illustrated by the 10 ways to dress like a Leo below.

Go for the Gold

#NotAllLeos. Luna is quick to point out that although not every Leo loves gold, many do. "As the Sun rules Leo, the color gold represents their regal and luxurious nature," she explains, adding that "Leos often gravitate towards gold accessories or clothing to showcase their self-confidence and desire for attention."

Play with Purple

"Associated with royalty, power, and creativity, purple is another color that Leos are drawn to," says Luna. "It represents their desire to stand out from the crowd and showcases their natural leadership qualities."

Find Luxe Fabrics

Unsurprisingly, "Leos love luxurious fabrics like silk and velvet that add a touch of glamour and elegance to their outfits," according to Luna. From metallic sequins to sheer, embroidered silk, and rich, crushed velvet, Leos love to make a statement not just with their clothes, but with what their clothes are made of.

See Red

"Leos, being natural-born leaders, are often attracted to red and the passion, energy, and strength associated with it as it complements their vibrant and enthusiastic personality. " Luna shares. Since Leos are also unafraid of fashion risk-taking, a head-to-toe red look could suit this fiery sign.

Opt for Orange

Another bold color beloved by this bold sign is orange. Luna explains that this energetic color symbolizes creativity, joy, and enthusiasm, three things that deeply resonate with the Leo spirit.

Lean into Luxury Labels

"Leos live for labels, luxury, and quality," Luna says. Don't be surprised to see Leos wearing decadent and over-the-top looks. While Leos are deeply attracted to all things luxurious, Luna says they're especially drawn to bold "designers like Versace, Chanel, Gucci, and any brand that makes logo monogram prints."

Try Tomato Girl

Of all this summer's TikTok fashion trends, Luna says that Tomato Girl suits the Leo fashionista best. "Leos are natural-born Tomato Girls," she says, citing La Bella Vita vibes, decadent gold rings and necklaces, silk scarves, and bold cherry red 'fits as tailor-made for this sign.

Pick Bold Patterns

Leos aren't afraid to draw attention to themselves with their outfits. In fact, you could say they crave it. Because they are also known for their sense of style and attention to detail, you'll frequently find them incorporating bold patterns into their looks, according to Luna.

Embrace Your Inner Baddie

"Leos believe they're the center of attention and they usually are," explains Luna, especially when it comes to making a statement. They're unafraid of showing off their sensuality, making bodycon dresses an ideal match for their baddie energy.

Accessorize with Statement Pieces

No Leo look is complete without roughly half a dozen bold and eye-catching accessories. "They really gravitate towards statement jewelry pieces, flamboyant handbags, stylish sunglasses, designer belts, and luxurious watches," says Luna. That said, Leos don't like bling for the sake of it. They want to stand out from the crowd and exude a sense of glamour and sophistication. As such, "Leos have a natural inclination towards high-quality and unique items that make a statement and showcase their refined taste," says Luna.

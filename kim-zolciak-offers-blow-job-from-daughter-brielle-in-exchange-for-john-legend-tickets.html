Kim Zolciak Offers Blow Job From Daughter Brielle in Exchange for John Legend Tickets

 Posted on April 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  309 words  |  Larita Shotwell

It's quite beneficial to live in a world where can tweet our favorite celebs in hopes of getting things like free concert tickets -- but "Real Housewives of Atlanta" star Kim Zolciak offered up a little more than she needed to.

On Wednesday night, the reality star tweeted at Chrissy Teigen to request tickets to her husband John Legend's show in the ATL. But instead of simply asking for tickets, she made a not-so-tasteful offer that Teigen quickly passed on.

"Sooo ur hubby is comin to ATL may19 & Kash is beyond OBSESSED w him! who does Brielle have to blow in order to meet him?? LOL," she tweeted, referring to her 20-year-old daughter Brielle Zolciak.

@chrissyteigen sooo ur hubby is comin to ATL may19 & Kash is beyond OBSESSED w him! who does Brielle have to blow in order to meet him?? LOL

@Kimzolciak

Teigen replied, "Plz don't blow anyone it's not that good of a show. Jk it's good but I'll get you tix without the oral."

@Kimzolciak Plz don't blow anyone it's not that good of a show. Jk it's good but I'll get you tix without the oral

@chrissyteigen

Of course, comments flowed on Twitter, with some calling out the "Real Housewives" star for being a terrible mother, while others simply noted that it was a terrible joke. Kim did not initially comment on her tasteless plea, instead she simply tweeted, "I love you @BrielleZolciak"

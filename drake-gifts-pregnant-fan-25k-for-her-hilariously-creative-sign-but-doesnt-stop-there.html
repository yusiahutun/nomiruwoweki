Drake gifts pregnant fan $25k for her hilariously creative sign - but doesn't stop there

 Posted on October 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  561 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Drake is at it again treating his fans on tour, and this time he’s gifted one pregnant fan a very generous sum of money.

The music star is currently embarking on his It’s All A Blue Tour – Big As The What? with fellow rapper J. Cole. In the past few months, Drake has gone viral with the way he shows his gratitude to fans, and one fan’s sign really got his attention.

Drake sends pregnant fan $25k

One lucky, and creative fan held a sign up at a recent show which read: “I’m five months pregnant, can you be my Rich Baby Daddy?”

The play on words alludes to the Toronto rapper’s hit song, Rich Baby Daddy on his latest album, which features Sexxy Red and SZA.

“Well, first of all, I don’t wanna offend your real baby daddy,” the 37-year-old responds as he sees the hilarious sign. His protective father then comes out as he says: “I’d love to first of all get you out the pits so we can put you somewhere safe like the VIP or some s***.”

“You can’t be pregnant and bouncing around when I start playing some of these slappers, we can’t have you getting pushed around.”

The fan then gets the shock of her life when the rapper announces: “Second of all, I’d love to give you $25,000 so you can be a rich baby mama,” which is of course, followed by huge cheers in the crowd.

$25k and a spot in VIP? Not a bad day in the office!

It’s not the first time the singer has given away mind-blowing gifts at his gigs. In August, Drake, real name Aubrey Drake Graham, went viral after gifting one lucky fan a $30k Birkin Bag.

The God’s Plan singer welcomed his only child, in October 2017. However, fans were unaware he was even a father until his album Scorpion, in June 2018. However, Pusha T revealed the star had a ‘secret son’ in May that year.

    Recipe: Dualit Pumpkin Spiced Latte (serves 1)

    INGREDIENTS:

    • 2 tsp All Spice (or 1tsp Ground Cinnamon, 1/2 tsp Ground Nutmeg and 5 Cloves)
    • 1 tsp Ground Ginger
    • 1/2 tsp Dememera Sugar
    • 250ml Cold Milk
    • 1 x Espresso Shot

    INSTRUCTIONS

    STEP 1

    To make the Pumpkin Spice Mix, add the cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, ginger and sugar into a pestle and mortar and grind until smooth. 

    STEP 2

    Place ¼ teaspoon of the pumpkin spice mix into a tall, latte glass.

    STEP 3

    Pour 250ml of cold milk into a milk frother, use a hand whisk or shake in a jar until double the volume. 

    STEP 4

    Make an espresso shot.

    STEP 5

    Pour the milk into the glass and stir in the spice. Allow to settle for one minute, before topping with your espresso shot.

    STEP 6

    Sprinkle another pinch of the Pumpkin Spice Mix on the frothy latte top for an Instagrammable finish! 

    Recipe: Vegan Pumpkin Spice Latte (serves 2)

    INGREDIENTS:

    • 4 cups Vita Coco coconut milk
    • ½ cup pumpkin puree
    • ¼ cup maple syrup
    • ½ tsp pumpkin spice mix
    • 2 shots of coffee (optional)

    To top:

    • Dairy-free whipped cream
    • A sprinkle of pumpkin spice
    • 2 x cinnamon sticks

    INSTRUCTIONS

    STEP 1

    Put all of the ingredients into a blender and blend together until smooth, then pour into a medium-sized pan and heat over a medium-high temperature until hot.

    STEP 2

    Divide between two large glasses and top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of pumpkin spice.

    STEP 3

    Serve with cinnamon sticks and enjoy!

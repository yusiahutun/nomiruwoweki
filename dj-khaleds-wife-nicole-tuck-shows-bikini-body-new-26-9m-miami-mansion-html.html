This week, camera's caught the Khaled clan soaking up the sun in south Florida while enjoying some water sports together out side of their new estate.

The radio personality and his long time love donned life jackets and climbed onto their home's personal dock to hop on a pair of Seadoo's.

Money must really be no object for the Weight Watchers spokesman. He was not only spotted using his cell phone on the dock, but he even brought it with him, seemingly unprotected onto the watercraft. 

The Wild Thoughts producer and his family recently moved into the 13,000 square foot home last week which he purchased for nearly $27m from Michael Lerner, who created the popular 'Baby on Board' signs in the 1980s.

Among the home's many features are nine bedrooms, 11 bathrooms, an 80-foot swimming pool and even an elevator.

'Breakfast vibe:' Khaled posted a low Weight Watchers point breakfast on Instagram on Thursday morning 

Ten months ago he embarked on the WeightWatchers program with plenty of fanfare.

'Man. WeightWatchers is going good,' Khaled told Page Six. 'It’s a real lifestyle because all you have to do is not go over your point [total]. And there are so many zero points [foods] in case you get hungry.'

His revelation saw stock at the company surge by 6per cent.

In recent weeks Khaled, real name Khaled Mohamed Khaled, has posted pictures of himself enjoying greasy meals with friends, sharing his last WeightWatchers post just over a month ago. 

On Thursday, the social media star took to Instagram to share 'a little breakfast vibe.'

The video showed a small bowl of fresh figs and bananas, next to three fried eggs and salsa with some green tinted tortillas. 

Eggs, salsa and fruit are incidentally all zero points on WW.

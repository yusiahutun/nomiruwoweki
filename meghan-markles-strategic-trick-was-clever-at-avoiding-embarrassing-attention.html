Meghan Markle's 'strategic' trick was clever at avoiding 'embarrassing' attention

 Posted on February 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  477 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Meghan Markle’s clever gesture at an event garnered praise from royal experts as they pointed out Kate Middleton’s influence on the Duchess of Sussex.

The of Wales, Kate Middleton has been commended by royal experts for her graceful and respectful gestures on multiple occasions. The 41-year-old royal member was recently praised for removing her shoes on a particular occasion, which touched fan hearts. Following in Kate’s footsteps, one gesture from Meghan Markle showed she also ‘adopted strategic tricks’ to avoid unnecessary attention.

Meghan Markle’s ‘strategic’ trick caught on camera

Meghan and Prince Harry postponed their royal honeymoon to attend a special party at Buckingham Palace to celebrate King Charles‘ 70th birthday in 2018. The garden party was a huge affair and all eyes were on the newlyweds. It came after reports of a royal feud within The Firm following the wedding of the couple.

As all eyes were on the former Hollywood star, she managed to keep eyes away, says one expert. The Duchess of Sussex wore a Goat Flavia nude shift dress with matching understated nude pantyhose and pumps. Along with her muted look, Meghan opted for a clutch and angled hat as her main accessories.

Experts loved how Meghan used her clutch as a small body barrier below her waist. But one expert revealed that there was more to the wardrobe staple.

Author and body language expert Judi James commented: “The clutch bag was a favorite of Kate Middleton’s and like Kate she carried it as a small body barrier just below her waist”.

She added: “That clutch bag positioning could be strategic. One annoying and embarrassing trait once you marry is that everyone starts asking when you’re going to have a baby and attention does start to focus on the tummy area.”

The duo had announced they were expecting their first child, Archie, months later. After tying the knot in spring of 2018, Harry and Meghan welcomed Archie the following year.

Meghan’s royal fashion evolved over the years

Meghan’s fashion has evolved over the years as she adopted more quiet luxury looks along with including sustainable fashion brands in her wardrobe.

One gown caught the eyes of fans, as the Duchess looked stunning on the red carpet. The former TV star wore a beige sleeveless turtleneck dress by sustainable California brand Bleusalt. She appeared in a video alongside Prince Harry as part of the Archewell Foundation’s initiative to advocate for a more equitable and accountable technology ecosystem.

But she also knows when to pull out all the stops. Meghan’s more glamorous look for 2023 included her golden dress at the Ms. Foundation Women of Vision Awards event.

She wore a strapless golden brocade Johanna Ortiz dress and golden Tom Ford heels at Ziegfeld Ballroom in New York City.

Vitamin C is a skincare powerhouse for a reason: It protects against free radicals, improves tone, texture, and radiance, and transforms skin with minimal irritation. If you ask any dermatologist or beauty editor, the ingredient is bound to have a spot in their morning routine (it also pairs so nicely with sunscreen). But vitamin C isn't perfect. It has a short shelf-life, and it's notoriously unstable.

Not to mention, most vitamin C serums I have encountered have an odd smell (like dirty pennies?) that can scare a skincare novice. So, I looked to Yon-Ka Paris for a solution to the drawbacks of vitamin C. The French skincare brand, which is favorite amongst well-heeled Parisians, recently launched a stable, super-potent vitamin C that smells so good.

How I Tested It

I started using this oil in early January and it lasted me over two months, despite using it every morning. Despite the oil texture, I slotted this vitamin C serum into my routine where I usually use the brightening ingredient: After a hydrating serum and before a moisturizer. But because this is an oil formula, I had to ensure it was the last product I applied before my moisturizer to avoid any pilling.

I started testing this serum in the thick of New York winter, so the luxurious feeling of the oil did wonders for my dehydrated skin. I used a dropper full every morning and took advantage of the slick texture by using the serum as slip for a facial massage. I massaged the serum until it fully absorbed into my skin before moving onto a moisturizer. As we move into warmer months, I still find myself reaching for the formula to give skin an extra layer of nourishment.

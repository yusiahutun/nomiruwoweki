Nancy ODell Biography

 Posted on July 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  304 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Nancy O'Dell is a television host as well as an entertainment journalist who has been known for being the co-anchor of the entertainment show Entertainment Tonight.

Early life & Education

She was born on the 25th of February, 1966 to Nancy Evelyn in Sumter, South Carolina.

Nancy was enrolled in the Coastal Academy High School for her primary education and graduated in the year 1990 with a degree in marketing from Clemson University. She has also been the winner of the Miss South Carolina present.

Journalism Career, TV works

Nancy O'Dell (age 50) is broadcast career started after she was called upon to become an anchor and reporter in WPDE-TV in Myrtle Beach, south Carolina. She then worked in the WCBD-TV as a crime reporter and morning news anchor before joining the WTVJ of the NBC in the year 1993.

Nancy rose up to fame after she joined the entertainment news program Access Hollywood in 1996. She also contributed to Dateline NBC as well as Today of the NBC news additionally. She joined Entertainment Tonight in the year 2011 and is a co-anchor of the show now.

Nancy has also appeared in several television shows like Charmed, Hannah Montana, and Brothers, etc.

Marriage and Divorce

Nancy belongs to white ethnicity and holds an American nationality. Talking about her personal life she got married to Dr. Richard O’Dell in the year 1995 but later the couple got a divorce after a 9-year relationship.

After the divorce, she again got married to husband Keith Zubchevich in the year 2005. The couple blessed with a baby named Ashby Grace in 2007.

Net Worth

Nancy has the current net worth estimated to be around $2 million dollars according to sources. She has been earning a good amount of salary in the range of thousands of dollars for being the host of Access Hollywood.

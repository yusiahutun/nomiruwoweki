Who is Jenicka Lopez?

Jenicka Lopez is a renowned reality television star and YouTuber. She was born on October 3rd, 1997, in California, in the United States, to Jenni Rivera and Juan Lopez. She was Jenni Rivera's fourth child and Juan Lopez's firstborn. Her family adored showbiz.

Jenni Rivera was a celebrated American-Mexican singer, songwriter, actress, entrepreneur, and spokesmodel. She was famous within the Regional Mexican music genre. CNN, Billboard, and The New York Times named her the most influential female in Mexican music during her career.

Jenni was a great inspiration to her daughter. Unfortunately, she met her untimely death in 2012. She died in a plane crash. Jenicka was left an orphan because her father, Juan, died in July 2009.

Selena Gomez walks on the wild side while holding hands with mystery man

 Posted on July 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  523 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Selena Gomez has been spotted holding hands with a mystery man, but if you blink you might miss it.

The Lose You To Love Me music hitmaker was seen sporting a skintight body-con dress in Paris. Listening to her animal instinct, the singer’s leopard print figure-hugging dress was paired with a long dark coat. However, as much as her fashion is usually show-stopping, some fans missed how she was holding hands with someone as she walked out of the building. The celebrity was watching a match between Paris Saint-Germain and Olympique de Marseille. Selena Gomez attended the game with star pals Nicola Peltz and Brooklyn Beckham at Parc des Princes.

Selena Gomez holds hands with mystery man

The stunning floor length number was a mesh dress by Ronny Kobo. Selena also opted for black pointed boots, her dark locks pinned up in a ponytail, as well as a small cross body black bag with a gold chain. Gold hoops and statement rings pulled the look together, and fans agree.

If you’re obsessed with her look, you can pick one up yourself. Although, it’ll set you back almost $400.

Despite how stunning the TV star looked, some onlookers may have disregarded the man she was walking with.

In the short clip below, you can see Selena walk out with a mystery man next to her, wearing a dark blue hoodie. At the beginning of the video, we can clearly see his hand is on her shoulder, with Selena holding onto his hand tight.

Then, when Selena sees adoring fans waiting to welcome her outside, she gracefully drops the hand-holding moment to walk over. The movie star then adorably poses for photos with her stans. They were patiently waiting to get a glimpse of the singer.

Selena confirms her dating status

It comes as the star appears to confirm her dating status for her followers on TikTok.

While wearing a white sweater, feeling fall cozy in all sorts of ways, Selena sits on a green couch and playfully holds up her pigtails.

She teases, ‘Guess who has a boyfriend?’, lip-syncing to a funny audio track. Then she shakes her head and mouths ‘Not me b****, be safe out there’.

At first, followers may have thought it was a cute announcement on a relationship for the Wizards of Waverly Place star. However, it turns out she’s just having fun, and we can now finally breathe. But the funny video was posted days before being spotted out in Paris – too soon to say?

Recently, she told SiriusXM Hits that she’s “enjoying” where she is right now.

She explained: “I think everybody goes through the phase of, ‘Oh, it’d be nice to like have someone,’ and I get that.

“But you know, I’m just enjoying where I’m at, and I just want to be happy with who I am so that whenever that person comes into my life, I can just have them add on to me instead of being this insecure, you know, person that I normally used to be.”

Grab your wallets and prepare to online shop 'til you drop!

Chip and Joanna Gaines's super popular Magnolia Market at the Silos is expanding its shopping area to include six new cottages, which will offer lifestyle products and goods specially curated by Joanna.

The new additions, which will be called the "Shops at the Silos," won't officially open until September, but you can shop a "small sampling" of their products online.

Joanna shared the good news and gave fans an exciting sneak peek of the new shops and their gorgeous collections.

"We are opening a set of six cottages that aims to offer [a shopping experience] that includes discovery, exploration and the small thrill of stumbling upon something that seems put there just for you," Joanna wrote in a new blog post on Magnolia.com. "Each retail cottage will have its own theme and unique perspective, and my sincere hope is that everyone who visits will find a niche that speaks to them. Whether you leave with a bag full of finds or simply a mind full of ideas, I hope a taste of the experience stays with you. And perhaps it will inspire you for years to come, as it did for me."

While the majority of the stores's products will only be available for purchase at Magnolia Market in Waco, Texas, Joanna said that "a small sampling" will be available online.

"When the shops are complete this fall, I do hope you can come stroll through each of them in person," she added.

Without further ado, the new shops include Chapter One, which sells books, paper goods, and chic office supplies like acrylic scissors and a concrete desk calendar; No. 16, which sells men's provisions like a sleek, multi-tool pen knife and a camouflage credit card wallet; and Tried & True, which sells jewelry and purses like Italian acetate hoops and a classic leather saddlebag.

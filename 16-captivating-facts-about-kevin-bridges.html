Rising Comedy Star

Kevin Bridges is a Scottish stand-up comedian who has quickly risen to fame in the comedy world.

Early Start

Born on November 13, 1986, Kevin Bridges began doing stand-up comedy at the young age of 17.

Unforgettable Debut

His debut solo show, “Kevin Bridges: Stands Up,” was performed at the Edinburgh Festival Fringe in 2007, where he received critical acclaim.

YouTube Sensation

Bridges gained significant attention when clips of his performances went viral on YouTube, garnering millions of views and helping to boost his career.

Record-Breaking Tour

Kevin Bridges holds the record for the fastest-selling comedy tour in Scottish history, with his “Kevin Bridges: The Story Continues” tour selling-out in just a matter of hours.

International Success

Television Appearances

Bestselling Author

Unfortunately, the relationship fails, and the man returns to the drawing board. After the breakup, the man meets his ex-girlfriend, looking better than before. So how do guys feel when they see their ex looking good?

Breaking up is never easy; it can make people bitter, resentful, and angry. Nobody ever knows when they will run into their ex. When a man sees his ex more attractive after a breakup, his feelings will depend on whether his love for his ex-partner was genuine.

How do guys feel when they see their ex looking good?

When do guys start missing their ex? Most guys never miss their ex. They are always happy that the relationship ended. But other guys start missing their ex within a week or a month. A guy may start missing his ex when he sees something or goes to a place that reminds him about her.

For some guys, the feeling of regret chips in when they see their ex looking good. A guy may regret it because he feels he should have held on to his ex and made the toxic relationship work.

Men usually compete for partners. Therefore, breaking up means they lost their partner to another person. Thus, he will become nostalgic and sentimental once he sees his ex-looking good. He will also acknowledge that he will not get back the good times he had with the girl.

2. He may want to get back together

Humans are different, and one relationship is not the same as the other. After a breakup, a man may want to move on. However, he may find the new relationship unexciting as the previous one.

5. Longing feeling

The feeling of longing emerges almost immediately after a breakup. Most guys begin to remember all the good things they experienced in their relationship. They regret how they treated their ex and now feel lonely with no partner to talk to.

When they finally see their ex-girlfriend looking good, they feel longing for their ex. The guys begin to associate the good time with their presence. That even forces them to forget why their relationship ended.

Not all men become emotional after ending their relationship with their ex-partners. Most men will be happy to see their ex looking good. Such men usually have no bad feelings for their exes because they have moved on.

9. Confused

A guy may feel confused when he sees his ex-girlfriend looking good. Initially, the guy thought his girlfriend was not working hard enough to look good for him. Therefore, he decided to end the relationship and look for someone better.

Suddenly, he sees his ex-girlfriend looking good, and many questions emerge. He wonders whether the girl ever loved him or was uncomfortable with him. But confusion also arises when the man is undecided on whether to rekindle the romantic relationship or let it die.

10. Lack of confidence

When a relationship ends, some guys may start questioning their self-esteem. That happens when the girl ends the relationship and not the man. The guy will lack self-confidence, especially if he cannot find someone else to replace her. He feels rejected and blames himself for what went wrong.

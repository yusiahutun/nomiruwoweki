Who is Ghostface Killah?

Ghostface Killah, born Dennis David Coles, is an American rapper and a hip-hop group Wu-Tang Clan member. He was born on 9 May 1970 in New York City, USA. Dennis is 53 years old (as of 2023), and his zodiac sign is Taurus.

Dennis Coles grew up in the Stapleton Houses housing project in Staten Island, New York City. He is the eldest among his siblings and has two younger brothers with the medical condition muscular dystrophy.

Ghostface has succeeded as a rapper with Wu-Tang and as a solo artist. He has 13 studio albums, five compilation albums and five collaboration albums.

Rihanna Savage x Fenty Lingerie Line Release Date, Photos, Sizes

 Posted on April 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  448 words  |  Martina Birk

Much fanfare is already surrounding Rihanna's much-anticipated Savage x Fenty lingerie line, even though she just announced the project two weeks ago. After massive retail success with both her rapidly-growing Fenty Beauty line and '90s-inspired athleticwear collection with Puma, it comes as no surprise that BadGal RiRi is expanding her empire.

In the past three years, the Barbadian singer has proven her impeccable ability to generate brand buzz and unprecedented revenue, without sacrificing diversity. Fenty Beauty just launched in September of 2017, but it's already on track to outsell Kylie Jenner's buzzworthy Kylie Cosmetics brand, which is two years old.

While we're still waiting on any official confirmation from Rihanna regarding the specifics of Savage x Fenty collection and retail partnerships, one thing's for sure—your future favorite bra could be a BadGal RiRi original. Below, we round up everything we know about the collection so far.

The collection launches on May 11.

It’s official: Savage x Fenty launches worldwide on Friday, May 11, which is in less than two weeks. The singer-turned-designer announced the launch date on Instagram with a sneak peek at one of her boudoir pieces—a sultry and sheer, pink floral bodysuit.

Early in March, speculation first began surrounding a possible Rihanna intimates line. In the weeks following the initial fashion retail rumors, it was radio silence from both the musician-turned-beauty-mogul and the supposed retailers.

But RiRi finally confirmed the rumors were true last month.

Plus sizes are included!

The songstress-turned-designer dropped some new smoking hot teaser images of the collection on the Savage x Fenty brand Instagram, announcing that the line will be inclusive, catering to both straight-sized and plus-sized women.

We already had an idea the size range would be diverse. Weeks ago, Mic pointed out that on the Savage x Fenty website, when fans were prompted to enter their email to get more info about the collection, they were also asked to enter their bra and underwear size. The list of options ranged from 32A to 44DDD for bras and XS to 3X in undies. “Other” is also an option in both categories.

The options served as a hint at the IRL size range for Rihanna’s lingerie collection, adding further proof that the BadGal values inclusivity. She did the same thing with her makeup line, Fenty Beauty, where she debuted 40 shades of foundation to encompass a diverse consumer base.

Best Silk Pillowcases for Wrinkles and Fine Lines

 Posted on April 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  386 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Your anti-aging arsenal is likely stocked up with plenty of serums or creams, but it’s not the only way to fight wrinkles. There’s one item in particular that you won’t find in the skincare aisle at the drugstore: We’re talking about silk pillowcases. Bedding for anti-aging? It seems a little bit out there, but switching up the fabric you sleep on can make a big difference when it comes to your skin. 

Why use a silk pillowcase?

Silk’s composition works in a way that other materials can’t. While traditional fabrics such as cotton and polyester can bunch up and press lines into your face, which causes wrinkles, silks reduce those crinkles and friction that can emphasize your fine lines, wrinkles, and blemishes. What’s more, it contains natural proteins and amino acids that help promote the production of collagen and elastin — two main ingredients for youthful-looking skin. Your hair will get a little extra TLC, too. Less friction means less knots, matted tangles, and frizzy hair. With the right silk pillowcase, you’ll be all prepared to combat signs of aging in your sleep and minimize bedhead in the morning — talk about a good night’s rest! 

How to choose best silk pillowcase?

A pillowcase’s silk quality is measured in something called momme, or mm. A higher momme number indicates a heavier weight. While you’re likely to pay a little more for a heavier silk pillowcase, it’s worth the investment, since it will still retain its original shape and condition, even after washing. In short? A pillowcase with a higher momme will last longer, giving you more bang for your buck.

Other than that, there aren’t many hard and fast rules when it comes to selecting a silk pillowcase. As long as it’s real silk as opposed to satin or a faux silk-like material, your hair and skin are sure to benefit.

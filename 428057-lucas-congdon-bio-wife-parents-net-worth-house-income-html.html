Educational background

Congdon studied at a local high school in Vermont the graduated from Montana State University in 1996 with a degree in Landscape Architecture. After that, he did another landscaping course in 1997.

Career history

Congdon realized his creativity and love for exterior design while curving and shaping stones in his teenage years. Two years after completing his education, the then-young man moved to Florida in 1999.

Florida's limestone rocks and different species of trees and plants inspired him to delve deeper into exterior designing.

Lucas joined FSPA & APSP and launched Lucas Lagoons company in 2003. His company's first project was to restore a picturesque natural sand bottom lagoon using limestone stones.

The project earned him a Gold Award in residential pool restorations and launched his success story. In addition, the designer's creativity and expertise have given him a steady flow of well-paying projects.

Hard Candy's Girl Scouts Makeup Looks &amp; Smells Like Cookie

Hard Candy is known for delivering high-quality products at sweet drugstore prices. And thanks to the brand’s new drop—a Hard Candy x Girl Scouts makeup collaboration—things just got a whole lot sweeter. The line consists of 10 brand new products, which are inspired by the Girl Scouts’ iconically delicious cookies. All of the products look like cookies, some of the products smell like cookies, and we can’t wait to get our hands on them. Even better, the products, ranging from a cookie-scented eyeshadow palette to a highlighter inspired by icing, are all available for $10 and under.

“With this collection, we’ve baked up the perfect treats to celebrate individuality, confidence, and creativity,” said Curan Mehra, CEO of Hard Candy. “We love creating collections our community desires, and this one is the sweetest.” 

This Hard Candy x Girl Scouts collaboration is tapping into our long-time love of sweet-scented makeup—bringing us back to the days of Too Faced’s beloved Chocolate Bar and Sweet Peach palettes. Whether it’s just plain nostalgia or actual obsession, I have fond memories of the time when the most popular makeup products were sweet-scented. Although it may be too much for some, scented makeup is just way more fun to apply. If you also have good memories of your Too Faced Semi Sweet Chocolate Bar Palette, you’ll know what I mean when I say sweet-scented products just hit different.

While some might say that scented makeup is too gimmicky, we couldn’t be more excited to cover ourselves in products that smell as good as they look. In a time when overpriced sophisticated products rule the shelves, Hard Candy is truly a breath of fresh air for making fun yet wearable makeup cool again. 

For a limited time only, the Hard Candy x Girl Scouts makeup collection is available on Hard Candy’s website and in Walmart stores. (You should also be able to shop the collection online at Walmart very soon!) Act fast, because these sweet-smelling products are having a moment—and we personally can’t wait to try all of them.

  • Cookie-Scented Shadow Palette

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Cookie-Scented Shadow Palette

    This cookie-inspired palette comes in three different variations: Thin Mints, Coconut Caramel (like Samoas or Caramel deLites), and Trefoil. Not only does it include 10 different shades—from matte to metallic and glittery—it also smells like your favorite Girl Scouts cookie!

  • Sweet Treat Lip Repair Oil

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Sweet Treat Lip Repair Oil

    If you can’t get enough of Girl Scouts Cookie scented products, the Sweet Treat Lip Repair Oil is another to add to your list. The lip repair oil is formulated with vitamin E and jojoba oil for intense hydration. And it’s available in Hard Candy x Girl Scouts’ scent trifecta: Thin Mint, Coconut Caramel, and Trefoil.

  • Get Ready Plush Headband

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Get Ready Plush Headband

    Get ready in style with this adorable plush headband, available in two styles: Cookie Trio and Trefoil. Inspired by Girl Scouts Cookies, it’s perfect for sweeping hair out of your face during makeup application.

  • Cookie Icing Face Highlighter

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Cookie Icing Face Highlighter

    This cookie-scented Icing Highlighter will be the cherry on top of your sweetest makeup look. It comes in two shades: Coconut Caramel (warm) and Trefoil (cool), and will add a pearly glaze to your look.

  • Cookie Glaze Lip Marker

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Cookie Glaze Lip Marker

    These creamy, buildable lip markers deliver your perfect lips in a swipe. The markers come in three scented shades: Thin Mint (a rich brown), Coconut Caramel (a warm brown), and Trefoil (a cool beige). Infused with vitamin E and shea butter, your lips will feel moisturized while looking great.

  • Refresh Mint Canvas Primer

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Refresh Mint Canvas Primer

    For a sweet base, this Refresh-Mint Canvas Primer smells like Thin Mints and will prep your skin for makeup. Containing niacinamide and hyaluronic acid, this primer refreshes the moisture of your skin so you’re putting your best foot forward.

  • Be Bold, Do More Powder Puffs

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Be Bold, Do More Powder Puffs

    These velour powder puffs will blend out your makeup in a flash. Each kit includes two puffs, which are ultra-soft and ready for action.

  • Hand Shake Press On Nails

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Hand Shake Press On Nails

    These Girl Scouts-inspired nail kits include 24 nails and a limited-edition sticker set. Made of 100% PCR, they promise to last at least 14 days of snacking on Girl Scouts cookies.

  • Cookie Batter Indulgent Volumizing Mascara

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Cookie Batter Indulgent Volumizing Mascara

    For those who love a brown mascara, Hard Candy’s Cookie Batter Mascara is sure to become a fan favorite. With a volumizing formula and an hourglass shaped brush to lift every individual lash, the mascara will leave your lashes looking sweeter than ever.

  • Hand Shake Nail Stickers

    Hard Candy x Girl Scouts Hand Shake Nail Stickers

    These super cute nail stickers are perfect for creating your very own custom Girl Scouts-inspired manicure.

Puberty Blues is a classic Australian coming of age story, but Sean Keenan's showed little maturity as he entered adulthood on Saturday.

He puzzlingly took to Instagram on Tuesday to share several pictures, including one of what appears to be his own bare privates with the rather crass hashtag of #birthdayballs.

Not surprisingly, the photos were quickly taken down - but not before they were spotted by CelebrityFix.

Always the quiet ones: Sean Keenan almost looks shy as he stands in front of the press; the Instagram photos were anything but

The Australian TV star posted other frames on social media that showed debauched celebrations with friends.

Clad in matching blue denim, his buddies shared a sloppy embrace while they kissed while exposing themselves.

These too were taken down almost instantaneously.

Adult blues? Sean seems much tamer in character on the Puberty Blues television series

For celebrities the rule has always been never drink and tweet but here Sean is proving that drinking and instagramming can be much, much worse.

Keenan lives in Western Australia and is most known for his role as Gary Hennessey in the 2012 television show Puberty Blues.

The show is based on the 1981 film of the same name, which in turn was an adaptation of a novel published in 1979, with season 2 expected to debut in early 2014.

A spokesperson for Keenan was not immediately available for comment.

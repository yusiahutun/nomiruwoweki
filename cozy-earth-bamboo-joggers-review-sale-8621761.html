I Cant Stop Wearing These Versatile Joggers From an Oprah-Loved Brand

 Posted on April 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  507 words  |  Martina Birk

When I love a pair of pants, I buy them in numerous colors. The Aritzia Conan Cropped Pant, for instance, hangs in my wardrobe in no fewer than seven shades; Amazon’s shopper-loved joggers, that function as both work and workout attire, I’ve bought in five hues. My next pants purchase? The Oprah-approved Bamboo Jogger Pant from Cozy Earth. 

I own the black pair — as does my best friend, who immediately bought her own after trying mine. For a limited time, you can shop them 20 percent off during Cozy Earth’s Spring Sale. Naturally, I’m snagging every color on discount while they’re still in stock.

The Cozy Earth Bamboo Jogger Pant is a lightweight, ultra-buttery, super-soft sweatpant that’s practical, versatile, and unbelievably comfortable. Available in regular and tall sizes, from XS-XXL, the joggers are size-inclusive. Choose among seven solid colors, each of which makes an excellent wardrobe staple. The tapered leg creates a svelte, streamlined fit that’s more elevated, and thus more versatile, than that of the average sweatpant: I’ve worn them with boxy, structured blouses, t-shirts, and athleisure tops. As my aforementioned friend says, they’re “the only sweatpants [she] would wear out to lunch.” 

Oprah deemed the joggers one of her favorite things in 2020, and has called out other picks from Cozy Earth over the past five years, including its shopper-loved, ultra-plush socks, also on sale for a limited time.

True to the name, the joggers are made from bamboo viscose — a material that’s softer than virtually any other fabric I’ve experienced. As it turns out, bamboo viscose is incredibly durable, too: I’ve worn the joggers countless times over the past year, and, despite washing them frequently, they remain pristine in look and feel. My friend, who has lamented how other dark sweatpants fade over time, shares that “the black stays true and vibrant,” on Cozy Earth’s, even after multiple washes.” The waist features an adjustable drawstring that’s fray-free, and gives the joggers a flattering, slip-resistant fit.

Furthermore, the pants are versatile from a seasonal standpoint: They’re designed with “all season versatility,” per the brand, which has proven accurate. Cozy enough for chilly evenings, they’re ultra-breathable and somehow equally comfortable to wear in warmer months. A true testament to their temperature-regulating nature: I, a sweaty sleeper, was surprised to find myself perfectly comfortable one night when I drifted off while wearing them.

Oprah, my best friend, and I are hardly alone in our love for these joggers. “The material is so soft and buttery,” says one reviewer, who calls the pants “literally the comfiest thing” they’ve ever owned. Furthermore, they say the fabric feels substantial “but doesn’t leave you feeling hot.” I couldn’t agree more. 

If you’re searching for the perfect, versatile, season-straddling jogger, look no further than the Cozy Earth Bamboo Jogger Pant. Hurry — the sale ends soon. I’m snapping up in more colors ASAP.

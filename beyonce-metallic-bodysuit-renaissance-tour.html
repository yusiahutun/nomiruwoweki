Beyoncs Metallic Bodysuit Might Be Her Coolest Onstage Look Yet

 Posted on January 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  294 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Beyoncé has already worn her fair share of head-turning designer costumes on her Renaissance World Tour—but that isn’t stopping her from introducing even more unforgettable looks onstage.

In photos shared to Instagram yesterday, the international icon debuted two brand-new looks she wore at her recent Barcelona show.

The first outfit put forth a sexy, futuristic take on the reemerging moto trend. Custom-made by Fendi, the body-hugging bodysuit featured a zipper down the center, as well as panels of metallic silver, crimson, and black. The suit came with matching thigh-high boots and a pair of fingerless silver opera gloves. The “Love on Top” singer accessorized the outfit in multiple ways at different points throughout the show, from layering it with a shaggy gray fur coat interspersed with streaks of black and red to topping it off with a metallic red wide-brim hat and black sunglasses.

For her second look, Beyoncé wore a custom Stella McCartney set. It consisted of a glittering chainmail long-sleeved top with a sculptural waist silhouette and matching leggings. She finished off the look with transparent vinyl pumps with crystal-embellished ankle straps.

Since the Renaissance tour started last month, Beyoncé has regularly taken to Instagram to roll out new onstage ensembles. So far, this has included a sparkling maroon minidress from Alexander McQueen, an architectural latex dress designed by Andreas Kronthaler for Vivienne Westwood, and a bee-themed dress and hat from Mary Katrantzou.

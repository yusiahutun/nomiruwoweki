The 8 Best Tall Floor Lamps for Rooms with High Ceilings

 Posted on February 6, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  926 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Lighting large rooms can be a challenge, especially when you have high or vaulted ceilings. Sconces mount to the wall and look beautiful, but require sawed holes and manual wiring to properly install. Fortunately, there’s an affordable, low-maintenance option that fills the void of dimly lit space: tall floor lamps.

If you want the effect of sconce lighting without the handyman, tall floor lamps (65-69 inches high) and extra tall floor lamps (70+ inches high) are the way to go. Scroll down for some of our favorites.

$93.99 (was $99.99), Wayfair

This globe-like floor lamp has a floor switch, so no more contorting yourself like a gymnast to turn on the light (especially if your lamp is placed behind a couch!). The glass orb gently filters light so you’re getting just the right brightness.

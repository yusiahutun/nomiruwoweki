Wondering where to watch the National Championship? Keep reading for the best and one of the only ways to stream it live for free this year.

The 2024 College Football Playoff National Championship—also simply known as the National Championship—will be held at NRG Stadium in Houston, Texas, on January 8, 2024. The game is the 10th College Football Playoff National Championship and will determine the nationall champion of the NCAA Division 1 Football Bowl Division for the 2023 season.

The game will be played between the Michigan Wolverines of the Big Ten Conference and the Washington Huskies of the Pac-12 Conference. Michigan are the winners of the Rose Bowl, while Washington are the winners of the Sugar Bowl. Both teams also played against each other at the 1993 Rose Bowl, which Michigan won. The 2024 College Football Playoff National Championship is also the first CFP National Championship game without a participant from the Southeastern Conference since the first championship in 2014, which featured Ohio an Oregon.

So where can football fans stream the College Football National Championship? Read on for how to watch the National Championship game live for free to see who wins between Michigan and Washington.

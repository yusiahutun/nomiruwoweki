Mariah Carey Looks Luxe in a Dramatic Fur Vest and Sequined Leggings

 Posted on March 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  332 words  |  Martina Birk

The Queen of Winter Mariah Carey is holding onto her title and continuing her style streak with more grand wintry looks during her annual getaway in Aspen, Colorado.

MEGA

Carey traded in her all-white outfit from Tuesday, for a monochromatic black look that is glitz and glam galore. The “All I Want For Christmas Is You” songstress stepped out on Friday night in a dramatic fur vest layered over a fitted ¾ sleeve shirt. To up the ante even more, she paired the pieces with matching sparkly sequined leggings and knee-high platform boots by Lououtin. It wouldn’t be a Carey outfit without black shield sunglasses and lots of diamonds, including a diamond tennis necklace, layers of diamond tennis bracelets, and several rings. For a final touch, the superstar wore a black-and-white knit beanie by Chanel over her long blonde locks.

MEGA

Like clockwork, Carey turned the Colorado streets into her runway again the next day. For the occasion, she bundled up in an oversized brown, red, black, and white plaid poncho by Burberry, paired with figure-hugging black leather pants. The pop star was dripping in luxury accents, including fuzzy brown earmuffs by Chanel, another pair of black sunglasses, and the same diamond accessories. This time, she styled her hair in perfect bouncy curls that were pulled back into a high ponytail.

Carey had herself a day of shopping at Louis Vuitton, Gucci, and Kith on Tuesday in head-to-toe icy white. Her ensemble consisted of a crisp white bodysuit tucked into a matching peplum miniskirt, which she paired with opaque tights and sherpa-lined knee-high platform boots. The star of the show, however, was Carey’s shiny cropped white Prada puffer jacket.

Carey’s longtime boyfriend, Bryan Tanaka, has noticeably been absent from the Aspen trip, amid rumors the two have split.

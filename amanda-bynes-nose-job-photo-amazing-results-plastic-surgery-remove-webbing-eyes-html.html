Troubled actress Amanda Bynes has took to Twitter to reveal she had a nose job.

The former star claims she went under the knife to remove 'webbed' skin that was growing between her nose and her eyes.

The 27-year-old star wrote: 'I had a nose job to remove skin that was like a webbing in between my eyes. Having surgery was the most amazing thing for my confidence!'

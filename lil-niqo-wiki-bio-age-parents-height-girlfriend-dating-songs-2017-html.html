Well, there's no doubt that many new rappers are emerging and today we will be talking about one of such prominent young figures. Indeed, it's no other than rapper Lil Niqo! 

Niqo is a young teenager rapper who has garnered fame amid the young generation, and it looks like he is here to stay! However, what goes beyond his rapping skills? Let's find out everything there is to know about Lil Niqo in his short wiki-bio along with his relationship status. 

Everything You Need To Know About Lil Niqo:

The 18-year old famous young rapper, Lil Niqowas, who was born in San Diego, CA, goes by the real name, Niqoles Heard. Although his parents' name remains undisclosed, Niqo shows his love for his parents on his social media too. 

On May 12, 2014, Niqo shared a picture of his mother on the occasion of Mother's Day and showed his gratitude towards his mother.

Niqo even earned his name as a 'new singing sensation' after he wowed the audience with his performance and carved the way to the stardom. After being noticed by Kevin Wales of 'Worldwide Entertainment', Niqo became the youngest hip-hop artist to be signed by a recording contract with Def Jam Records.

In the year 2011, Niqo released his debut single 'Ok, Then' featuring DJ Khaled.

Niqo performed in 'The Youngster Cypher', in the year 2012 which was organized by Black Entertainment Television (BET). Niqo's career reached new heights after he released his second single named, 'I Know' in the year 2016. But, amongst the list of his songs, the most popular single is 'Television Love' released in 2012 which helped him gain a wide recognition amid the youngsters. 

Recently on April 1, 2017, Niqo released his latest single called, 'The City'.

By the looks of it, he would continue to charm his fans with his performances.

Lil Niqo's Dating Status:

Just like many celebs who prefer to keep their personal content to themselves, Lil Niqo is no different! He has concealed the details of his love life, and least information regarding his romantic unions have surfaced the media.

However, back in 2012, Niqo uploaded a tweet confirming that he doesn't have a girlfriend and even said that his focus was his career.

Meanwhile, he has constantly been seen with dancer, Faith Thigpen and fans are speculating the duo to be dating. On May 3, 2016, Niqo mentioned Faith as 'my girl' via his tweet. 

On September 16, 2016, Pilgrim Studios also tweeted stating that Niqo and Faith made a cute couple. 

 Additionally, Niqo and Faith have also been attending events together and even sharing pictures on social media too. Niqo shared a photo with Faith at an event back in March 2017.

Nonetheless, it is yet to be officially confirmed from both ends, so the fans have to wait until either of two come sheds light on the nature of their relationship.

Daniel Caesar Plots Case Study 01 North American Tour

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Martina Birk

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) –R&B singer, songwriter, and artist Daniel Caesar announced plans for a North American tour to follow the release of his new full-length EP Case Study 01, which hit the shelves today.

The North American segment of Caesar’s Case Study 01: Tour kicks off on July 30th with a performance in Honolulu and extends through November before wrapping up on Nov 28th with a performance in Vancouver.

Before he starts his North American run, Caesar is lined up for a series of dates in Indonesia and Asia, starting on July 20th in Jakarta, with additional dates set for Manila, Kuala Lumpur, Bangkok, Nagata, and Incheon.

Special guest Koffee joins Daniel Caesar on select North American dates.

Tickets for Caesar’s US dates go on sale to the general public beginning July 2 at 12pm local time and July 5th for Canadian performances.

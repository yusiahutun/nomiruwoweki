The Evolutionary Line

Natu is a Psychic/Flying-type Pokémon that belongs to the evolutionary line starting with Natu and evolving into Xatu.

The Dream Pokémon

Natu is often referred to as the “Dream Pokémon” due to its ability to foresee the future by observing the celestial bodies.

Psychic Powers

Known for its powerful psychic abilities, Natu can communicate telepathically with others of its kind and even control their dreams.

Small and Nimble

Natu may be small in size, but it compensates with its impressive agility and quick movements, making it a difficult target to hit.

Mysterious Origins

The origins of Natu are shrouded in mystery. It is believed to have descended from ancient Pokémon that lived in dense forests.

Nature’s Alarm Clock

Natu has a unique capability to accurately sense changes in the Earth’s magnetic fields, acting as a natural compass and alarm clock.

Solar Powered

Natu draws energy from the sun to fuel its psychic powers and maintain its vitality. It enjoys basking in sunlight during the day.

Hollow Soul

Legend has it that when Natu sleeplessly stares into the night sky, its body is filled with mysterious power, as if its soul becomes hollow.

Dawn Chirps

Early morning is when Natu is most active. It lets out a unique chirping sound that is considered a sign of good fortune and new beginnings.

Aerial Acrobat

Natu is an excellent aerial acrobat, effortlessly performing impressive flips, twists, and turns in the sky while flying at high speeds.

Dual Vulnerabilities

As a Psychic/Flying-type Pokémon, Natu is weak against Rock, Electric, Ghost, Ice, and Dark-type moves.

A Lone Observer

Natu is often seen standing still and gazing intently at something in the distance, as if it is observing the world with a profound wisdom.

Perceptive Vision

With its keen eyesight, Natu can detect even the faintest movement from a great distance, a skill that helps it stay alert and avoid danger.

A Superstitious Pokémon

Natu has several superstitions surrounding it. Some believe that encountering Natu brings good luck, while others see it as an omen of doom.

Nesting Habits

Natu builds its nest using small twigs and leaves high up in trees. Its nests are constructed meticulously and are known for their durability.

Communal Gatherings

Natu is known to gather together in groups during significant celestial events, such as solar eclipses and meteor showers.

Controlling Dreams

Through its psychic abilities, Natu has the power to enter the dreams of others, providing comfort or sometimes causing nightmares.

Xatu’s Prophecies

According to legends, Xatu, the evolved form of Natu, has the ability to see into the future, offering glimpses of what is to come.

The Unsettling Gazes

When faced with danger or during battles, Natu’s eyes can emit an intense glare that is said to paralyze foes with fear.

A Symbol of Balance

Natu and its evolution, Xatu, are seen by some as symbols of balance between the past, present, and future, bridging the gap between worlds.

These 20 facts about Natu highlight the unique characteristics and abilities of this enigmatic Pokémon. From its psychic powers to its role as a dream predictor, Natu remains a captivating and mysterious creature in the world of Pokémon.

FAQs

1. How can I obtain a Natu in Pokémon?

To obtain a Natu, you can search for it in the wild grass of various locations, such as Ruins of Alph, the Safari Zone, or the Lost Cave in Pokémon Gold and Silver. You can also trade for a Natu with other trainers or breed it from a parent Natu or Xatu.

2. What are Natu’s primary strengths?

Natu’s primary strengths lie in its psychic abilities and its flying type, which gives it an advantage against Fighting, Bug, and Grass-type Pokémon. It also has a decent Special Attack stat, making it a capable offensive Pokémon.

3. Can Natu learn any unique moves?

Yes, Natu can learn a few unique moves, such as Future Sight, which allows it to attack with delayed damage, and Night Shade, which inflicts damage based on its level. These moves can be useful in battle and add to Natu’s special abilities.

4. What is the significance of Natu’s ability to see into the future?

Natu’s ability to see into the future is based on its lore, which suggests that it is connected to ancient wisdom and knowledge. While this ability may not have a direct impact in battles, it adds to Natu’s overall mystique and makes it an intriguing Pokémon.

5. How does Natu evolve?

Natu evolves into Xatu at level 25. Once it reaches this level, it will undergo a transformation and gain more powerful abilities as it becomes the majestic Xatu.

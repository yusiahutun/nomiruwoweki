Who is Joey Graziadei?

Graziadei was born on 24 May 1995 in Royersford, Pennsylvania, meaning he is 28 years old as of 2024. Joey Graziadei's dad is Nick Graziadei, while his mother is Cathy Pagliaro. His parents divorced when he was in kindergarten, shortly after his dad came out as gay.

Graziadei grew up alongside his two sisters, Eleanor and Carly Monzo. Eleanor graduated from Palm Beach Atlantic University in May 2019.

Joey Graziadei's educational background

The American reality TV personality graduated from Spring-Ford High School in 2013. He later moved to West Chester University, earning his bachelor's degree in communication and media studies in 2017. While in the institution, he represented the Pi Kappa Alpha fraternity.

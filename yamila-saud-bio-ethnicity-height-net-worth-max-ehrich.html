An aspiring actress since young, Yamila has been performing on stage since she was seven years old. So, to further enhance her talent, Yamila studied at the famous Academy of Carlos Gandolfo, alongside Dora Baret and Matias Gandolfo, and it helped her kick-start her career in TV and film production.

Later, she then went on to graduate from the New York Film Academy (NYFA) — one of the premier visual and performing arts colleges in the world.

As of 2022, she has appeared in movies and TV shows including An American Masquerade (2022), El Encanto (2020), Solo el Amor (2018), Hypersomnia (2016), Farsantes (2013), and Condicionados (2012).

Besides acting, Yamila has also stepped into the industry as a producer in Solo el Amor (2018), Recreo (2018), and Hypersomnia (2016). However, the actress had no plans to be a producer. “When I started to produce I didn’t know it all, but it just happened naturally,” she explained.

Talking about her accolades, Yamila won the “Best Actress in Peru” at the “Insólito”, the Terror and Fantasy Film Festival in Lima. Also, She was selected for a special screening at the Blood Window Galas at the 2017 Cannes Film Festival.

Taylor Swift's friend reveals timeline of breakup song speculated to be about Joe Alwyn

 Posted on October 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  446 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Taylor Swift has finally released Midnights’ Vault track You Are Losing Me digitally on all streaming platforms, and fans believe this song could be about her breakup with Joe Alwyn.

For Swifties, Midnights, aka Taylor Swift‘s tenth studio album, has been a treasure trove where the music legend dropped new surprise features even months later with the Till Dawn edition. One track from the album, which was only available on the CD version, is You Are Losing Me.

The heart-wrenching track became available worldwide on November 29, 2023. Since then, fans have been theorizing that, like most Taylor songs, it holds a major clue to her relationship with British movie star Joe Alwyn, which ended at the beginning of 2023.

Taylor Swift’s friend and producer ‘drops clues’ about Joe Alwyn split

Taylor Swift’s relationship with Joe Alwyn was mostly away from the cameras, but the 34-year-old celebrity gushed about her beau on several occasions, including in her Grammy acceptance speech when she talked about how much she enjoys writing songs with him.

The relationship that fans thought would be The 1 ended after six long years and, since then, has been a topic of discussion among swifties. Fans have come up with many theories regarding the breakup’s timeline. With the release of You Are Losing Me, the Internet is piecing together the puzzle of Tay’s heartbreak.

As per Tay Tay’s close friend and producer Jack Antonoff’s Instagram share, the singer-songwriter wrote You Are Losing Me in December 2021, long before calling quits with Joe Alwyn.

The ’emotional breakup’

Taylor is known as the queen of heartbreak anthems which resulted from her personal relationships with a slew of Hollywood heartthrobs. It is no surprise that each and every song of Taylor gets the microscopic treatment where fans try to decipher what stage of the singer’s life could make her write yet another banger with universally relatable lyrics.

After learning the timeline of You Are Losing Me, fans are convinced that Taylor’s relationship with Joe went through several phases of loss and the Mirrorball crooner has depicted the crucial stages perfectly in her latest song.

“You’re losing me is NOT a post-breakup song. It’s a song written during a time where you feel like the relationship is GOING to end but it hasn’t yet, when you’re begging your partner to DO SOMETHING to show that the relationship isn’t over,” a fan wrote on X (formerly Twitter).

Another echoed: “the way it’s worded “you’re losing me”, she had never written that after the breakup. that had been going for YEARS!”

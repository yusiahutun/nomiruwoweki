  • Hilary Duff married Matthew Koma on December 21, 2019.
  • Duff has now shared an intimate look at the wedding gown she wore for her nuptials.
  • The stunning ivory dress was created by Jenny Packham.

Hilary Duff pulled off a secret, at-home wedding to Matthew Koma last week, and the bride has finally shared some snaps of her beautiful gown.

Duff wore an ivory dress by Jenny Packham for her nuptials, featuring a fishtail skirt and what Packham referred to as "empowered shoulders," also known as saddle shoulder-pads. Meanwhile, the caped sleeves featured a split in each arm, and culminated in an impressive train. Kate Middleton often wears Jenny Packham designs, and it's easy to see why.

The Lizzie McGuire star wore her hair simply, with a slight wave, and accessorized the look with a delicate headband featuring diamonds and pearls.

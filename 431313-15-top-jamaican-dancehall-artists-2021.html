Many of the best dancehall artists rose to fame during the 1970s, but the movement and genre is still growing. From the origins of the genre to the top hits of the current year, here are the top Jamaican dancehall artists of all time.

1. Vybz Kartel

Vybz Kartel may be in prison, but his music is still being heavily played. The dancehall artiste was sentenced to at least 32 years and six months for the murder of Clive Lizard Williams. He has, however, never stopped releasing music ever since he was taken to prison in 2011. Vybz Kartel fans have never been starved as there is always new music from the artiste.

2. Rytikal

Newschool dancehall fans are familiar with the work of Rytikal. The young artiste has been busy throughout 2021, making new music and even releasing visuals for old audios. Rytikal is known for songs like Where Im From, We Okay, and Chosen. He is 25 years old.

3. Popcaan

Popcaan has been striving to be king of Jamaican dancehall ever since his debut single. Apart from shining in Jamaica, Popcaan knows how to expand his fan base by securing international collabos. Popcaan has worked with artists in America, the UK and Africa.

The Party Shot hitmaker has been in the industry for about two decades, and he continues to reign supreme. The latest 2021 Popcaan songs include Survivor, Win, and Relevant.

4. Alkaline

Ever since Alkaline became a household name in the Jamaican dancehall scene, fans and critics observed that he was trying to copy Vybz Kartel. From his style of singing to his physical appearance, Alkaline tried to follow how Kartel moved. The artiste has since curved his path and continues to make music that fans enjoy. The latest alkaline songs include Deh Suh and Sell Off.

5. Koffee

Koffee has been ruling airwaves for the past five years. The 21-year-old Jamaican singer first came into the limelight after a song she uploaded on Instagram went viral. She later released her first single, Legend, with just her voice and acoustic guitar in 2017.

Today Koffee is one of the most prominent female dancehall artists. He never stops releasing singles and collaborating with others in the industry.

6. Beenie Man

Beenie man is a renowned veteran in dancehall. One of the best dancehall singers, Beenie Man, began his career in 1979 and has been consistent through the years. Also known as The Girls Dem sugar, Beenie Man shows his musical prowess by performing in live gigs and collaborating with both new artists and legends like himself. Some 2021 songs by Beenie Man include Self Love, Dem Aguh, and Fun in the Sun, featuring Popcaan and Dre Island.

7. Intence

Born Tashawn George Gabbidon, Intence is a 26-year-old dancehall artiste from Tavern in Kingston 6. Intence tries to be versatile in his music and always strives to bring something different for his fans. Some artists hits include Bad Gyal, One Pants, and Live Yuh Life. The latest records from Intence are Yah Now and The Fight.

8. Konshens

Every Konshens fan will agree that one thing you have to do in this life as a dancehall fan is attend one of his concerts. Konshens is always full of energy both in the studio and when performing live.

The Gal A Bubble and Bruk Off Ya Back hitmaker has been releasing hits since 2005. At 36, Konshens still has the energy to perform and even make better music for his fans.

9. Dexta Daps

35-year-old Dexta Daps is famed for his dancehall songs as well as ballads. Born Louis Grandison, the singer is known for songs like Weak to You, Chinese Jordan, Miss You So Much, and Be High. His latest songs include Wifi and Im on My Way.

10. Spice

Spice has been the ultimate queen of dancehall for the longest time. She started her career in 2000 and gradually rose to be the most adored female dancehall artist. Spice never misses on her songs.

She is also a deejay, record producer, actress, and entrepreneur. The latest pieces from Spice are Go Down Deh, Send it Up, Money Walk, and Watch My Life.

11. Bounty Killer

If you are a dancehall fan who grew up in the 90s, you know that Bounty Killer was the most sought after and one of the most aggressive reggae dancehall artists in his prime. Bounty is known for his hardcore lyricism, street boy talk, and that rude look.

His music is distinct, and that is why the 49-year-old still receives his flowers as a musician today. Bounty Killer is still releasing music in 2021. Some of the latest songs are Bang Bung and Where You Come From.

12. Govana

Govana, who also goes by Deablo, is among the top Jamaican dancehall artists in 2021. The artiste is a prodigy of Aidonia, another dancehall king. Together with Aidonia, he has been working on major projects and expanding his catalogue. His latest songs include POP, 1Matik, Karen, and Chap chop.

13. Shenseea

25-year-old Shenseea is a Jamaican dancehall recording artist, singer, and songwriter. Having been in the industry for less than a decade, Shenseea is doing pretty okay for herself as her fan base grows while she records new music.

Shenseeas first song was Jiggle Jiggle, which was released in 2016. Her latest songs are Be Good, Run Run, and Bad Alone.

14. Aidonia

Aidonia has been in the game since 2004. The Fi di Jockey singer has been releasing hits and albums widely played worldwide and is one of the best Jamaican artists today.

15. Shane O

Shane O had his first major hit when 17. He released Lightning Flash and Crab Inna Barrel while still a teenager, making him a favourite for many at the time. Shane O, however, experienced a career slump at some point and never released any good music.

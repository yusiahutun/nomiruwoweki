Bella Hadid Modeled Lingerie While Sitting Backwards On a Chair

 Posted on February 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  276 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Bella Hadid is ringing in the holidays doing what she does best: gifting us with a sexy Instagram post. On Monday, the supermodel took to Instagram to show off a BTS photo from her latest shoot for Victoria's Secret.

Hadid sat backwards on a gold chair facing a giant fan positioned by hairstylist Mustafa Yanaz as she wore a black-and-silver mesh thong and matching bustier. She paired the lingerie set with drop earrings and butt-length, pin-straight hair while she held a little glass bottle of water.

She shared that the snapshot was from the set of a Victoria's Secret photo shoot. "The days we have on set are some of the best days I've had ... @mustafayanaz VS Soccer Tournament 2022 ...," she wrote. ".. Made possible by a few of my favorite angel humans @raulmartinez1024 @felicitybwebb ♥️♦️♥️."

The company has recently undergone a massive rebrand, during which they ditched the Angels and recruited a diverse group of celebrities and activists, like Megan Rapinoe, Priyanka Chopra Jonas, and Poloma Elsesser, to lead the VS Collective initiative.

In a recent interview with Marie Claire, Bella explained her reasoning behind coming back to the brand, following alleged sexual harassment behind the scenes.

"What magnetized me to coming back was them coming to me and really proving to me that, behind the scenes, Victoria's Secret has changed so drastically," she said. "There was a type of way that, I think, a lot of us women who used to work with Victoria's Secret felt. And now, six of the seven [VS] board members are all female. And there's new photoshoot protocols that we have. So a lot has changed."

Recipe for Chick-fil-A pizza goes viral on TikTok

 Posted on February 24, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  867 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Experimenting with fun recipes has been one of the most popular activities for many people in quarantine - and now there's a new recipe that many may want to try out: a Chick-fil-A pizza that has taken TikTok by storm. 

The dish first started trending on the social media app a few months ago, however it has seen a resurgence in recent weeks as users in lockdown have sought new recipes to try out while self isolating at home. 

For those who are able to get their hands on the fast food favorite during quarantine, the pizza is incredibly easy to make - but it is, according to those who have tried it, the recipe yields a very delicious dish. 

A tasty treat: TikTok users have gone gaga over a recipe for Chick-fil-A pizza 

Easy as can be: The recipe calls for a store-bought pizza base, 12 Chick-fil-A nuggets, one large order of waffle fries, shredded mozzarella, and several packets of Chick-fil-A sauce

Delicious! In order to make the pizza, simply cover the dough in Chick-fil-A sauce, then top that with your cheese, before adding the chicken and waffle fries on top 

Just a handful of ingredients are required for the fast food creation: a store-bought pizza crust, shredded mozzarella - or an alternative cheese of your choice - 12 Chick-fil-A chicken nuggets, one large order of waffle fries, and several tubs of the chain's signature sauce. 

You then put your pizza dough on a suitably sized, pre-prepared baking tray or a pizza stone, rolling it out if necessary. 

Enjoy! TikTok user @mattchodey was one of hundreds of people to try out the recipe

Top the dough with Chick-fil-A sauce; this essentially replaces the tomato sauce on a traditional pizza, so ensure that the entire base is covered in a layer of it, before topping that with your mozzarella. 

Then, chop up your chicken nuggets into even smaller chunks - either into halves or thirds - and do the same with the waffle fries, ensuring that they are all cut into bitesize pieces.

Spread the chicken and the fries across the pizza, ensuring that they cover the entire thing, and that there is a good mix of both toppings in each area. 

Then, bake the whole thing in the oven for ten minutes at 450 degrees Fahrenheit. 

When it has turned slightly brown on the top, and the cheese has all melted, pull it out of the oven and prepare to enjoy. 

You can add some extra flavor by drizzling more of the sauce over the top of the pizza, or you can keep some on hand to dip your crust into while eating the finished pie.

Dozens of users have tried their hands at creating the delicious dish, with the majority going gaga over the finished product.

How to make TikTok's viral Chick-fil-A pizza

Ingredients  

  • 1 store bought pizza base 
  • Shredded mozzarella or alternative cheese of your choice 
  • 12 Chick-fil-A chicken nuggets 
  • 1 large Chick-fil-A waffle fries 
  • Several packets of Chick-fil-A sauce 

Method 

Put the pizza base on suitably-sized, pre-prepared baking tray or pizza stone and cover the dough in Chick-fil-A sauce. 

Cut up the nuggets and the fries into bitesize chunks.  

Top the sauce with your shredded cheese. 

Add the nuggets and the fries on top of the cheese. 

Place the pizza in the oven for 10 minutes at 450 degrees Fahrenheit. 

Enjoy!

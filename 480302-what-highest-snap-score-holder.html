What is a snap score?

The Snapchat score is essentially a tool for tracking your in-app activities. This means that it shows the total number of photos you have sent to your friends while using the app. However, nobody truly knows the exact factors that Snapchat's algorithm uses to determine your snap score.

The app will consider various other factors besides the photos you have been sending and receiving on Snapchat. It will, for instance, keep track of how many stories you've posted and how many people are on your friend list.

20 Dog-Walking Outfit Ideas Inspired by Celebrities

 Posted on July 2, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1036 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Who knew dog-walking could be so chic? Even though the everyday task is as routine as they come, your outfit doesn't have to be. Cue A-listers who know that even the most ordinary outing is rife with street style potential.

Emma Stone was polished to perfection in a charming blue schoolboy blazer, pale-pink brogues and a chunky knit beanie while walking Ren, her and beau Andrew Garfield's Golden Retriever, in New York City. Gwen Stefani took a more glam approach, standing out with Pomeranian Chewy in a red-hot shift dress that matched her signature red lip. Blake Lively accompanied a frolicking Baxter (hubby Ryan Reynolds' dog) in a casual-chic fall-ready look, topping it off with a moto jacket, a Gucci carryall and ankle-boots. See all 15 stylish pup-loving stars who are living proof that a girl's best accessory is her dog.

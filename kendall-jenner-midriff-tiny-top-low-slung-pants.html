Kendall Jenner Bares Midriff in Low-Slung Pants and a Tiny Top

 Posted on July 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  323 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Kendall Jenner is offering a new take on the low-rise pants trend.

A few days ago, the supermodel showed her off-duty style in a casual look while walking around New York City. Instead of wearing pants with a waistband that drops to her hips—an early-aughts trend that's made a comeback in street style looks of fellow models—Jenner opted for a creamy pair of skin-clinging trousers with a V-shaped waistband, which dipped right below her belly button.

The 818 Tequila founder stayed warm in a roomy caramel suede jacket. Other accessories included a pair of Gucci shades, a white shoulder bag, and brown heeled boots.

MEGA

The model recently wore a dramatic goth look to the 2022 Met Gala. The extravagant Prada gown featured a sheer fishnet bodice with floral details and a showstopping skirt that reflected the extravagance of the Gilded Age with a colossal train and ruched adornments. And, for added effect, Jenner also bleached her eyebrows.

ANGELA WEISS//Getty Images

Kendall was joined by the rest of her sisters—Kylie Jenner, and Kim, Kourtney, and Khloé Kardashian—as well as Kardashian matriarch Kris Jenner at the glitzy affair. The family unit has been busy promoting their new Hulu reality TV series, The Kardashians.

In a recent BAZAAR.com interview, Kendall opened up about her sister Kourtney's upcoming nuptials to her fiancé, Travis Barker.

"I have a secret psychic ability that no one knows about," she said. "I always had a feeling [that they would be together]. I would encourage her every day. I'd be like, 'Just do it. Just try. Take it to the next level!'"

Kansas City Chiefs owner Clark Hunt's socialite daughters Gracie and Ava jet off on LAVISH vacation

 Posted on July 15, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1287 words  |  Martina Birk

Kansas City Chiefs owner Clark Hunt's socialite daughters Ava and Gracie Hunt spent the first few days of 2024 soaking up the sun in Cabo San Lucas on a very lavish vacation - where they stayed at a five-star resort and attended a private Jonas Brothers concert.

The sisters have both become viral social media sensations for flaunting the many glamorous perks that come with being NFL heiresses - like getting to hang out with Taylor Swift and embarking on over-the-top vacations - so it should come as no surprise that they kicked off the New Year by heading off on yet another glamorous getaway.

Ava, 17, and Gracie, 25, headed to Mexico with their parents, football mogul Clark, 58, and former beauty queen Tavia Hunt, 52, to celebrate the holiday.

They stayed at the luxurious Hilton Los Cabos Beach & Golf Resort - where rooms cost at least $500-a-night during the busy months - and enjoyed a slew of luxurious amenities and VIP treatments.

Ava has also been showing off her jet-set endeavors online for years, but it was only recently that she was thrust into the spotlight - thanks to her new-found friendship with pop sensation Taylor.

The two met at a Chiefs game in December - Taylor was there watching her boyfriend, Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce - and when Ava posted a picture that showed her and the Cruel Summer songstress, 34, smiling in the VIP suite together afterwards - it launched her into online fame.

High school student Ava has followed in her mother and big sister's footsteps and she competed in the Texas Outstanding Teen pageant back in 2021.

But it seems her main passion is cheerleading - back in 2020, she came in first place at the cheerleading State Championships. 

Last year, she was picked as her school's varsity cheer captain, and she's even said online that she dreams of one day being an NFL cheerleader for her dad's team.

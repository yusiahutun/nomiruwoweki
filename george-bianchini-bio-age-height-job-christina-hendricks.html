Meet George Bianchini, Christina Hendricks Boyfriend-Turned-Fiance

Christina Hendricks and her cinematographer boyfriend George Bianchini are engaged. The American actress announced their engagement via an IG post. She captioned the post, “We proposed to each other and we said yes!!! I will love and care for him forever. @steadig.”

The two fell in love while working together on the NBC television series Good Girls, which ran from 2018 to 2021. Bianchini served as the show’s camera operator while Hendricks played “Beth” in the comedy-drama series.

DailyMail’s relationship first went public in November 2021. When Christina and George visited the SCAD Museum of Art’s presentation of fashion designer Christian Siriano’s “People Are People” exhibition on October 22, 2021, in Savannah, Georgia, they appeared to be dating.

During the event, Christina and George cozied up to one another as they posed for some photos during a tour of the reception area of the exhibition, which was presented by The SCAD Museum Of Art in Savannah.

The Hollywood star hasn’t been afraid to share photos of her boyfriend online, uploading the first one of them together in March 2022. When the two were spotted together at Christian Siriano’s “People Are People” exhibition in 2021, relationship suspicions first surfaced.

The couple’s announcement was met with celebratory comments from friends. “Yayayayayayay!!!!!❤️❤️❤️,” wrote her Mad Men costar, January Jones. “Howbow dat?! Congrats y’all!” Good Girls‘ Retta commented. “YAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAY Mr and Mrs Seagull!!!!!!!” Hendricks’ Good Girls onscreen sister Mae Whitman wrote.

“Queer Eye” star Tan France also showed his support for the happy couple, commenting, “OMG!!!!! I’m so, so happy for you both!!! ❤️”

Christina was previously married to actor Geoffrey Arend. They announced their separation in 2019.

“Twelve years ago we fell in love and became partners. We joined our two amazing families, had countless laughs, made wonderful friends, and were blessed with incredible opportunities,” Christina wrote at the time. “Today we take our next step together, but on separate paths.”

“We will always be grateful for the love we’ve shared and will always work together to raise our two beautiful dogs. We will be taking time to rediscover ourselves in this transition and we thank you for your patience and support in giving us the space to do so,” Christina added.

They were reportedly introduced in 2007 by Vincent Kartheiser.

George Bianchini Job

George Bianchini worked as a cinematographer and camera operator for films, Television, Commercials, Music Videos & Live Productions. According to LinkedIn, he worked at STEADI-G since August 1999. Furthermore, he worked at STEADI SYSTEMS CORP as STEADICAM/ CAMERA OPERATOR. George has more than 12 years of experience.

Besides TV shows, George has also worked on commercials such as O2 Mobile f/Beyonce, Baileys, Cialis, Nowness, CVS, Samsung, Aetna, Reuters, L’Oreal, UPS, Avon, Panasonic, GHI, Mercedes-Benz, Colonial Williamsburg, Coca-Cola, and several other brands.

George completed his associate’s degree from Santa Fe Community College. He earned BFA from University of Central Florida in 1992. Moreover, he earned MFA in Photography from Temple University in 1995.

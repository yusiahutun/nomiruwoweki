Sarah Jessica Parker's latest outfit for And Just Like That... season two filming has been slammed by fans pining for Carrie Bradshaw's iconic style.

The actress, 57, posed up in a pinstriped Marrakshi Life dress, a Vivienne Westwood teddy long coat, paired with blue boots from her own faux brand Duchessa Gardini, a grey fedora and a vintage Pierre Cardin bag  - with unimpressed fans declaring the star looked like a 'fancy hobo' in the outfit.  

The wardrobe for the HBO Max show is now designed by Molly Rogers and Danny Santiago - and has come under serious scrutiny after fashion icon Patricia Field, who styled Sex And The City for its six seasons and the two movies, declined to return due to her work on Emily In Paris.

9 best lilac dresses for summer 2023: Slinky midi dresses, floral dresses, mini dresses &amp; more

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1100 words  |  Martina Birk

Lilac is definitely having a moment in 2023, and we're seeing so many gorgeous dresses in the pretty pastel shade. Whether you're looking for the perfect dress for wedding season or you're in need of a staple style to reach for all year, lilac is the shade to look out for.

We've seen everyone from Princess Kate to Rihanna rocking a lilac dress, and the versatile colour will make a statement whilst still being versatile enough to style with a range of footwear and accessories.

From floral pieces for the brunch date in your diary to slinky midi dresses for your next date night, scroll on to find the best lilac dresses for any occasion this summer.

How to style a lilac dress

Injecting a subtle pop of colour into your wardrobe, a lilac dress will look great paired with so many other shades, and it's flattering on almost every skin and hair tone. The pastel colour is particularly lovely during the summer months, and we've seen tons of lilac dresses being worn this wedding season. When dressing for an occasion, we'd style a lilac dress with a pair of white strappy heels, or for a more laid-back outfit, we recommend opting for a pair of beige espadrille sandals to complete the look. 

How we chose the best lilac dresses

  • Price: We chose a range of price points for every budget, ranging from high street to luxury styles. 
  • Style: We've selected lilac dresses in a range of styles for different occasions, from versatile midi dresses to flattering mini dresses, all of which are suitable for summer.
  • Trusted brands: While we haven't tried on all of the products included, every trusted brand is loved by myself or members of the HELLO! team. 

Best lilac dresses for summer

  • River Island Frill Lilac Dress

    Editor's Note

    If you're looking for the perfect wedding guest dress for the summer, this is it. The flattering frill trim will have you feeling your best all day – and lilac midi would look gorgeous teamed with a pair of bright pink heels and a matching clutch bag.

    River Island's lilac wrap dress features the prettiest frill detailing, with a sweetheart neckline, a figure-flattering belt, and a subtle polka dot print. The waterfall skirt has a sheer finish, with a satin underskirt to complete the look.

  • Nobody's Child Frill Lilac Dress

    Editor's Note

    This Nobody's Child midi is at the top of my wishlist right now. I love the scoop back teamed with the ruffles – and I would style the dress with a pair of lilac espadrilles to match.

    Romantic ruffles, a slim V-neck, and an empire waistline make up Nobody's Child's lilac dress - and we love it. The fitted waist and thigh-high slit will complement any figure, and the open back perfectly rounds off the summer style. 

  • River Island Broderie Lilac Dress

    Editor's Note

    We'd style River Island's broderie dress with a pair of white trainers and a denim jacket for an effortless daywear look, but the textured midi can be styled with just about any footwear for an ultra-feminine ensemble.

    Broderie dresses are a timeless summer trend, and River Island's wrap midi is perfect for daytime wear. It features a V-neck, delicate ruching around the waist, and lightly puffed short sleeves. It's available in sizes 6-18, and it also comes in, yellow, black and white.

  • M&S Floral Lilac Wrap Dress

    Editor's Note

    This Gina Bacconi floral dress is another great option for wedding guest dressing, but it would also be great for wearing to garden parties. The romantic tiered frill beautifully elevates the dress for the most elegant finish - and it may be worth investing in if you want a summer dress that you can easily dress up.

    A floaty silhouette, a pretty floral print and a flattering V-neck, what's not to love about Marks & Spencer's blooming lovely midi dress?  

  • Reformation Halterneck Lilac Dress

    Editor's Note

    Reformation's halterneck lilac dress is an absolute showstopper, and we can totally see it being worn as a bridesmaid dress.

    Reformation's stunning lilac dress ties in at both the neck and the waist, enhancing the silhouette perfectly. It comes in a maxi length, but the thigh-high slit elongates the leg, particularly if worn with a pair of heels.
